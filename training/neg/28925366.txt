Human iPSC line IISHDOi001-A was generated from fibroblasts of a patient with
McArdle disease harbouring the mutation, c.148C>T; p.Arg50Ter, in the PYGM gene. 
Reprogramming factors Oct3/4, Sox2, Klf4, and c-Myc were delivered using Sendai
virus.
