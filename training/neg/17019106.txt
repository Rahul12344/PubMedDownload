
1. Curr Opin Anaesthesiol. 2001 Jun;14(3):299-306.

Medical and obstetric problems complicating pregnancy.

Hoesli I(1), Louwen F, Holzgreve W.

Author information: 
(1)University Women's Hospital, Basel, Switzerland. ihoesli@uhbs.ch

The present review addresses several medical and obstetric factors/problems, and 
their interactions during pregnancy. These include the following: maternal age;
morbidity and mortality during pregnancy; thromboembolism; gestational diabetes; 
haemolysis, elevated liver enzymes, low platelets syndrome; human
immunodeficiency virus; hepatitis C; preterm labour; antenatal administration of 
corticosteroids; intrapartum surveillance; breech delivery; and caesarean
section.
