
1. Zh Mikrobiol Epidemiol Immunobiol. 1988 Aug;(8):69-72.

[Epidemiologic effectiveness of mass revaccination against measles].

[Article in Russian]

Deshevoĭ SE, Rykushin IuP, Ovsiannikova IV, Malykhina RM, Pogorelyĭ IuA.

The data on the epidemiological effectiveness of the mass booster immunization of
students of high schools, as well as professional and technical schools, against 
measles are presented. Booster immunization is considered to be a highly
effective antiepidemic measure aimed at decreasing morbidity rate in measles.
