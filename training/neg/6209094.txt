[Structural organization of the Epstein-Barr virus genome].

[Article in Russian]

D'iachenko AG.

The properties and structure of Epstein-Barr virus (EBV) DNA are described. The
restriction endonuclease maps of the viral genome are presented. Differences in
the structure of DNAs from various subtypes of EBV and their influence on the
phenotype status are discussed.
