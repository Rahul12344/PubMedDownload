
1. Infect Immun. 1978 Apr;20(1):321-3.

Bluetongue virus, an exceptionally potent interferon inducer in mice.

Jameson P, Schoenherr CK, Grossberg SE.

The attenuated American BT-8 strain of bluetongue virus is 5 to 10 times more
potent an interferon inducer than any other viral or nonviral agent reported to
date, including as much as 600,000 units/ml of plasma by 8 h after intravenous
injection.
