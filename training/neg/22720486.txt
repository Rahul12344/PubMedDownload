How to prevent the spread of norovirus.

McGeary T.

Norovirus has gained notoriety as the winter vomiting bug. While the infection is
usually self-limiting, its impact on healthcare resources is significant. This
article advises nurses how to maximise infection control strategies during an
outbreak.
