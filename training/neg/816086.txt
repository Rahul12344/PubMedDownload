
1. Vopr Virusol. 1976 Jan-Feb;(1):26-32.

[Human D(B) type virus: comparison of D(B) type virus antigens with antigens of
oncornaviruses of different origins].

[Article in Russian]

Il'in KV, Oroszlan S, Bova D, Zhdanov VM, Gilden R.

It was found by the immunodiffusion test that the D(B) type virus isolated from
human cell lines and Mason-Pfizer monkey virus (M--PMV) contained common
group-specific antigen(s). No cross-reaction was demonstrated between this D(B)
type virus and C type oncornaviruses of various origins.
