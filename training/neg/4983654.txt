
1. J Bacteriol. 1970 Jan;101(1):330-2.

Incorporation of uridine into Bacillus subtilis and SPP1 bacteriophage
deoxyribonucleic acid.

Barlati S.

Tritiated uridine is incorporated into the deoxyribonucleic acid of Bacillus
subtilis and bacteriophage SPP1; the tritium is recovered in the cytidine moiety 
of both deoxyribonucleic acids.
