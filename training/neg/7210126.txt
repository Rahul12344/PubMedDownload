
1. Trans R Soc Trop Med Hyg. 1980;74(6):743-4.

Rubella immunity in Nigerian children.

Odelola HA, Familusi JB, Akinyemi A.

A serological survey was carried out to determine the rubella
haemagglutination-inhibiting (HI) antibodies among Nigerian children. The
subjects consisted of 100 neonates and 550 children aged one month to 15 years.
The study shows that rubella immunity rose with increasing age of the children.
