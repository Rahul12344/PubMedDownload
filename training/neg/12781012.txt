Severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) in Singapore: clinical features of index
patient and initial contacts.

Hsu LY(1), Lee CC, Green JA, Ang B, Paton NI, Lee L, Villacian JS, Lim PL,
Earnest A, Leo YS.

Author information: 
(1)Tan Tock Seng Hospital, Singapore. liyang_hsu@yahoo.com

Severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) is an emerging viral infectious disease.
One of the largest outbreaks of SARS to date began in Singapore in March 2003. We
describe the clinical, laboratory, and radiologic features of the index patient
and the patient's initial contacts affected with probable SARS.
