
1. Arch Virol. 1990;115(3-4):309-16.

Entry of pseudorabies virus into CHO cells is blocked at the level of
penetration.

Sawitzky D(1), Hampl H, Habermehl KO.

Author information: 
(1)Institute for Clinical and Experimental Virology, Free University of Berlin,
Federal Republic of Germany.

Replication of pseudorabies virus (PrV) in Chinese hamster ovary (CHO) cells, a
cell line naturally resistant to infection by herpesviruses, is blocked at the
level of penetration. Virions bound to the surface of CHO cells are taken up into
cytoplasmic vesicles and degraded.
