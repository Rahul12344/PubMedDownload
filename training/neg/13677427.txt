Secondary glomerulonephritides.

Naicker S(1).

Author information: 
(1)Division of Nephrology, Department of Medicine, University of the
Witwatersrand, Parktown, Johannesburg, 2193, South Africa.
naickersd@medicine.wits.ac.za

This review of the secondary glomerulonephritides outlines presentation clues to 
assist the primary healthcare worker in making the diagnosis. Glomerulonephritis 
(GN) due to the following disorders will be described: hepatitis B virus (HBV),
hepatitis C virus (HCV), human immunodeficiency virus (HIV), sickle cell disease 
(SCD), and systemic lupus erythematosis (SLE).
