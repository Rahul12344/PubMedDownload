Importation of Mumps Virus Genotype K to China from Vietnam.

Liu W, Deng L, Lin X, Wang X, Ma Y, Deng Q, Xue X, Zhong G, Jin L.

During May-August 2016, mumps virus genotype K was detected in 12 Vietnam
citizens who entered China at the Shuikou border crossing and 1 girl from China. 
We provide evidence that mumps genotype K is circulating in Vietnam and was
imported to China from Vietnam.
