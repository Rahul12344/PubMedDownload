Genome sequencing sheds light on emerging drug resistance in malaria parasites.

Neafsey DE(1).

Author information: 
(1)Broad Institute, Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. neafsey@broadinstitute.org

Comment on
    Nat Genet. 2013 Jun;45(6):648-55.

Plasmodium falciparum in Southeast Asia are gradually becoming resistant to
artemesinin, a standard first-line treatment for malaria. Whole-genome sequencing
offers a chance to better understand and perhaps undermine the parasite's
evolutionary response to this drug.
