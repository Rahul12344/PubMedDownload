Variations in human herpesvirus type 8 seroprevalence in Native Americans, South 
America.

Souza VA(1), Salzano FM, Petzl-Erler ML, Nascimento MC, Mayaud P, Borges JD,
Pannuti CS.

Author information: 
(1)Universidade de São Paulo, São Paulo, Brazil.

To determine the epidemiology of human herpesvirus type 8 (HHV-8) among
non-Amazonian native populations, we conducted a cross-sectional study in Brazil,
Bolivia, and Paraguay. Our data show striking ethnic and geographic variations in
the distribution of HHV-8 seroprevalences in Amazonian (77%) and non-Amazonian
native populations (range 0%-83%).
