Glycopolypeptides of rubella virus. Brief report.

Toivonen V, Vainionpää R, Salmi A, Hyypiä T.

Purified rubella virus particles contain two glycopolypeptides (62K and 44K to
51K) and one nonglycosylated polypeptide (35K). Glycoproteins can be labeled with
tritiated sodiumborohydride after oxidation with galactose oxidase indicating
that galactose is the terminal carbohydrate unit. The other carbohydrate
components are N-acetyl-D-glucosamine and mannose.
