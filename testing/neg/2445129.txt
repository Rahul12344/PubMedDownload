
1. Zh Mikrobiol Epidemiol Immunobiol. 1987 Jul;(7):21-3.

[Primary serological screening of the infection caused by the LAV/HTLV-III/HIV
virus in Moscow].

[Article in Russian]

Pokrovskiĭ VV, Iankina ZK, Toporovskiĭ LM, Ryvkina VG, Pokrovskiĭ VI.

The screening covering 5,155 residents of Moscow and 6,412 foreign students
revealed the presence of antibodies to the virus, causing immunodeficiency in
man, in 20 students from African countries. The antibodies were detected by means
of the immune blotting assay. The problem of the possibility of the heterosexual 
transfer of the virus is discussed.
