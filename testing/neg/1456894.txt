
1. Arch Virol. 1992;127(1-4):321-5.

Tick-borne encephalitis virus interaction with the target cells.

Maldov DG(1), Karganova GG, Timofeev AV.

Author information: 
(1)Institute of Poliomyelitis and Viral Encephalitis, Moscow, Russia.

The binding of tick-borne encephalitis virus to porcine kidney embryo cells was
studied. Anti-idiotypic antibodies against TBE virus E protein precipitated gp110
kDa which is predicted to be a cellular receptor for TBE virus.
