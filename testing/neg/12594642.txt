Sexually transmitted diseases other than human immunodeficiency virus infection
in older adults.

Calvet HM(1).

Author information: 
(1)Long Beach Department of Health and Human Services, Long Beach, CA 90815, USA.
hecalve@ci.long-beach.ca.us

Sexually active older adults may engage in activities that put them at risk for
sexually transmitted diseases (STDs). A brief review of the main STDs among older
adults, including the epidemiology, clinical presentations, and diagnosis of and 
treatment recommendations for such STDs, is presented.
