Nucleotide sequence of RNA 3 of the British type isolate (Blencowe strain) of
tomato aspermy virus.

O'Reilly D(1), Thomas CJ, Coutts RH.

Author information: 
(1)Advanced Technologies Cambridge Ltd., UK.

The complete sequence of RNA 3 of the Blencowe, British (B) isolate of tomato
aspermy virus (TAV) is presented. The RNA 3 of TAV-B is dicistronic, encoding the
putative movement protein 3a and the capsid protein.
