[Diagnostic image (116). A man with acute exanthema. Epstein-Barr virus

A 19-year-old man presented with a rash, several days after antibiotic therapy
for pharyngitis. Such a rash is highly suggestive of an acute viral infection;
Epstein-Barr virus infection was confirmed serologically.
