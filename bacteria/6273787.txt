Restriction endonuclease EcaI from Enterobacter cloacae.

Hobom G, Schwarz E, Melzer M, Mayer H.

Restriction endonuclease EcaI obtained from Enterobacter cloacae DSM30056
recognizes the group of heptanucleotide palindromes 5'-G[unk]G-T-N-A-C-C-3', and 
on cleavage (arrow) produces fragments with 5'-terminal pentanucleotide
extensions. It is identical in specificity with restriction endonuclease BstEII
from Bacillus stearothermophilus ET.
