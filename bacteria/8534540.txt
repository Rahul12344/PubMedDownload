[Liver abscess caused by Streptococcus anginosus].

[Article in Spanish]

Gil Lasa I(1), Montalvo Ollobarren II, Mensa Landaburu G, García Bengoechea M,

Author information:

Pyogenic liver abscess is a relatively rare disease, often concerning elderly
patients in bad general condition and with underlying diseases. We report the
case of a 77-year-old female with gastric cancer and liver abscesses by
Streptococcus anginosus.
