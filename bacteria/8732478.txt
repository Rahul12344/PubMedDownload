HLA-G polymorphism and allelic association with HLA-A in a Finnish population.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Medical Microbiology, University of Oulu, Finland.

The polymorphism of the HLA-G gene can be identified by PCR-RFLP analysis. This
short communication describes the PCR-RFLP analysis of HLA-G polymorphisms in
exons 2 and 3 and the association of different HLA-G and HLA-A alleles in 26
healthy Finnish families.
