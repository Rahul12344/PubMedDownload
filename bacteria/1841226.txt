
1. Parassitologia. 1991 Dec;33 Suppl:349-51.

Susceptibility tests on Phlebotomus perniciosus and Phlebotomus perfiliewi wild
populations in Sicily.

Lavagino A(1), Ansaldi G.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Hygiene and Microbiology, University of Palermo, Italy.

In Sicily, wild populations of Phlebotomus perniciosus and P. perfiliewi were
submitted in 1990 to WHO method for susceptibility to DDT, Malathion and
Permethrin. P. perfiliewi has shown less susceptibility than P. perniciosus,
mainly to DDT.
