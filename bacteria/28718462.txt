[Preventive dentistry 7. Getting rid of halitosis].

[Article in Dutch; Abstract available in Dutch from the publisher]

de Jong TMH, Laine ML.

Halitosis or bad breath is a problem that affects many people and its source is
generally found in the oral cavity (intra-oral halitosis). Bacteria in the oral
cavity produce volatile sulphur compounds such as hydrogen sulphide and methyl
mercaptan that not only emit an unpleasant odour, but may also have toxic effects
on the periodontal tissues. Good oral hygiene, a healthy periodontium and healthy
teeth are the basis for the prevention of intra-oral halitosis and dentists and
dental hygienists therefore play an essential role in its prevention.
