mSphere of Influence: Positive Research Culture Enables Excellence and
Innovation.

Ballou ER(1).

Author information: 
(1)Institute of Microbiology and Infection, School of Biosciences, University of 
Birmingham, Birmingham, United Kingdom e.r.ballou@bham.ac.uk.

Elizabeth Ballou works in the field of medical mycology. In this mSphere of
Influence article, she reflects on how two papers by Okagaki et al. (PLoS Pathog 
6:e1000953, 2010, https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.ppat.1000953) and Zaragoza et
al. (PLoS Pathog 6:e1000945, 2010, https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.ppat.1000945) 
made an impact on her career by demonstrating an alternative to destructive
publication practices.
