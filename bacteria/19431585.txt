A birefringence relaxation determination of rotational diffusion of magnetotactic
bacteria.

Rosenblatt C, Frankel RB, Blakemore RP.

The orientational relaxation of the magnetotactic bacterium Aquaspirillum
magnetotacticum is observed by the decay of the optical birefringence upon
switching off an aligning magnetic field. The data yield a rotational diffusion
constant D(r) [unk] 0.13 s(-1) and information about cell sizes that is
consistent with optical microscopy data.
