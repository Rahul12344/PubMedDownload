Leukocyte migration: scent of the T zone.

Cyster JG(1).

Author information: 
(1)Department of Microbiology and Immunology, University of California San
Francisco 94143-0414, USA. cyster@itsa.ucsf.edu

Recent studies show that the chemokine receptor CCR7 helps T lymphocytes and
dendritic cells to navigate into the T-cell zone of lymphoid organs. Furthermore,
down-modulation of CCR7 redirects some activated T cells from these zones into
other lymphoid tissue compartments.
