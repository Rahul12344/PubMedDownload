The Signal Recognition Particle Database (SRPDB).

Larsen N(1), Zwieb C.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Microbiology, Michigan State University, East Lansing 48824,
USA.

The Signal Recognition Particle Database (SRPDB) provides aligned SRP RNA and SRP
protein sequences, annotated and phylogenetically ordered. The current release
included 93 RNAs and 29 proteins representing SRP9, SRP14, SRP19, SRP21, SRP54,
SRP68 and SRP72. The SRPDB can be downloaded and is accessible via the World Wide
Web.
