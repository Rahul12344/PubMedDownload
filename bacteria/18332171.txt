Mouse beta-defensin-14, an antimicrobial ortholog of human beta-defensin-3.

Searching the database for mouse homologs of the antimicrobial peptide human
beta-defensin-3 (hBD-3) revealed highest identity (69%) to mouse beta-defensin-14
(mBD-14). Recombinant mBD-14 exhibited broad-spectrum, nanomolar microbicidal
activity. Treatment of keratinocytes with gamma interferon or transforming growth
factor alpha increased mBD-14 gene expression. These data suggest that mBD-14 is 
the functional ortholog of hBD-3.
