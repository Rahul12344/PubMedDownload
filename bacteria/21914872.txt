
1. J Bacteriol. 2011 Oct;193(19):5548. doi: 10.1128/JB.05707-11.

Complete genome sequence of Leuconostoc kimchii strain C2, isolated from Kimchi.

Lee SH(1), Jung JY, Lee SH, Jeon CO.

Author information: 
(1)School of Biological Sciences, Chung-Ang University, 221, HeukSeok-Dong, Seoul
156-756, Republic of Korea.

Leuconostoc kimchii strain C2 was isolated from fermented kimchi in Korea. Here
we announce the complete genome sequence of Leuconostoc kimchii strain C2,
consisting of a 1,877,174-bp chromosome with a G+C content of 37.9% and no
plasmid and describe major findings from its annotation.
