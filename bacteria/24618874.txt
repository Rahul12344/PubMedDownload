
1. Chem Commun (Camb). 2014 Apr 21;50(31):4089-91. doi: 10.1039/c4cc00647j.

A novel P450-based biocatalyst for the selective production of chiral 2-alkanols.

von Bühler CJ(1), Urlacher VB.

Author information: 
(1)Institute of Biochemistry, Heinrich-Heine University Düsseldorf,
Universitätsstraße 1, 40225 Düsseldorf, Germany.
Vlada.Urlacher@uni-duesseldorf.de.

A P450 monooxygenase from Nocardia farcinica (CYP154A8) catalyses the stereo- and
regioselective hydroxylation of n-alkanes, still a challenging task in chemical
catalysis. In a biphasic reaction system, the regioselectivity for the
C2-position of C7-C9 alkanes was over 90%. The enzyme showed strict S-selectivity
for all tested substrates, with enantiomeric excess (ee) of up to 91%.
