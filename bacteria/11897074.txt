
1. Emerg Infect Dis. 2002 Feb;8(2):202-3.

Prosthetic valve endocarditis caused by Bartonella quintana.

Klein JL(1), Nair SK, Harrison TG, Hunt I, Fry NK, Friedland JS.

Author information: 
(1)Hammersmith Hospital, London, United Kingdom.

We describe the first case of Bartonella quintana endocarditis affecting a
prosthetic valve in a person with no known risk factors for this infection.
Bartonella should be considered as a cause of endocarditis in any clinical
setting.
