Variable expression of O:61 in Salmonella group C2.

Mikoleit M(1), Van Duyne MS, Halpin J, McGlinchey B, Fields PI.

Author information: 
(1)Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, National Center for Emerging,
Zoonotic, and Infectious Diseases, Division of Foodborne, Waterborne, and Enteric
Diseases, Enteric Diseases Laboratory Branch, Atlanta, Georgia, USA.
MMikoleit@cdc.gov

According to the Kauffmann-White scheme, 39 pairs of serovars in Salmonella
serogroup C2 differ only by the minor antigen O:6(1). We characterized strains
from 10 serovars representing five Salmonella serogroup C2 pairs. All strains
demonstrated variable expression of O:6(1). These results indicate that these
pairs are not distinct serovars.
