
1. Science. 1966 Nov 18;154(3751):893-5.

Chlorinated hydrocarbon pesticides: degradation by microbes.

Chacke CI, Lockwood JL, Zabik M.

In culture, most of the actinomycetes and filamentous fungi tested degraded PCNB;
several actinomycetes dechlorinated DDT to DDD, but no microorganism degraded
dieldrin. Streptomyces aureofaciens degraded PCNB to pentachloroaniline.
