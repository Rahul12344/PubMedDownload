Intestinal irony: how probiotic bacteria outcompete bad bugs.

Comment on
    Cell Host Microbe. 2013 Jul 17;14(1):26-37.

In this issue of Cell Host & Microbe, Deriu et al. present a mechanistic
explanation underlying the benefits of certain probiotic bacteria. Intestinal
bacteria compete for the essential nutrient iron, leading to replacement of
pathogenic Salmonella by the probiotic Escherichia coli Nissle, which is better
equipped with iron acquisition systems, and resolution of infectious colitis.
