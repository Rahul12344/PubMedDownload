Comment on
    Cell Host Microbe. 2015 Jun 10;17(6):741-51.

Malaria parasites must express a large contingent of invasion-related genes to
propagate within host red blood cells. In this issue of Cell Host & Microbe,
Josling et al. (2015) identify a bromodomain protein that serves as a
transcriptional activator required to coordinate expression of these genes and
enable parasite proliferation.
