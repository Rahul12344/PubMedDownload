[Research advance in antitumor activities of artemisinin and its derivatives].

[Article in Chinese]

Yang H(1), Tan XJ.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, PUMC Hospital, Beijing, China.

Artemisinin and its derivatives are highly effective in fighting against malaria.
Notably, these drugs have shown potent anti-timor activities by arresting
cellular growth, enhancing apoptosis, inhibiting angiogenesis, and regulating the
expression of tumor-associated genes, although the underlying mechanisms remain
unclear.
