
1. Emerg Infect Dis. 2015 Mar;21(3):490-2. doi: 10.3201/eid2103.140898.

Novel mutations in K13 propeller gene of artemisinin-resistant Plasmodium
falciparum.

Isozumi R, Uemura H, Kimata I, Ichinose Y, Logedi J, Omar AH, Kaneko A.

We looked for mutations in the Plasmodium falciparum K13 propeller gene of an
artemisinin-resistant parasite on islands in Lake Victoria, Kenya, where
transmission in 2012-2013 was high. The 4 new types of nonsynonymous, and 5 of
synonymous, mutations we detected among 539 samples analyzed provide clues to
understanding artemisinin-resistant parasites.
