The article considers the problem of access to stomatological care, in particular
to prosthetic dentistry, for people with limited health capacities in conditions 
of the COVID-19 pandemic. The range of factors impeding to get quality dental
services in state stomatological polyclinics and at home was established by
empirical study organized in two Subjects of the Russian Federation located in
the Central Federal Okrug.
