Covid-19 pneumonia: An unusual radiological presentation.

A 26 year old female presented with complaints of high grade fever and cough for 
10 days. Nasopharyngeal swab tested for COVID-19 RT-PCR at admission was
negative. Clinical examination suggested a patch of bronchial breathing in left
infrascapular region and bilateral diffuse rhonchi. Chest X-ray was suggestive of
left lower zone consolidation. HRCT showed a large patch of consolidation with
GGO along with a cavitary lesion involving left lower lobe. Sputum for RT-PCR
COVID 19 was positive. Patient was managed as per covid-19 protocol, subsequently
showing clinical and radiological improvement.
