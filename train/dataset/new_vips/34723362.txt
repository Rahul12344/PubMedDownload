Leading a Hospital Incident Command System during a global pandemic.

Maurer ML(1).

Author information: 
(1)Patient Care Services, and the Cynthia and Robert J. Lepofsky Chief Nursing
Officer, Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center, Boston, Massachusetts.

Responding to a healthcare crisis such as COVID-19 requires a practiced, nimble
incident command structure. Our medical center is in one of the areas in the US
that was hardest hit by the initial wave. Effective leadership of incident
command was critical in our response.
