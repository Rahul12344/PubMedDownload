Ischemic cholangiopathy following hepatic artery pseudoaneurysm with thrombosis.

Borao Laguna C(1), Aso Gonzalvo MC(2), Lorente Pérez S(3).

Author information: 
(1)Aparato Digestivo, Hospital Clínico Universitario Lozano Blesa , España.
(2)Aparato Digestivo , Hospital Clínico Universitario Lozano Blesa .
(3)Aparato Digestivo, Hospital Clínico Universitario Lozano Blesa .

We report the case of a 73-year-old male, with a history of liver cirrhosis
secondary to hepatitis C virus (HCV). Due to this condition he underwent a liver 
transplant (LT) in 1993. From then on he was given immunosuppressive treatment
with cyclosporin in monotherapy.
