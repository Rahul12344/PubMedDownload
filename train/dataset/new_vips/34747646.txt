COVID-19 pandemic: Real-world experiences and lessons learned.

Srijithesh PR(1), Hakim SH(2).

Author information: 
(1)National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences, India.
(2)27243University Hospital Zurich, Switzerland.

The COVID-19 pandemic has disrupted all walks of life on an unprecedented scale. 
In the following report, we recount the experience of stroke and
neurointerventional practitioners from different parts of India. It was written
in September 2020 when the first wave of the pandemic was at its zenith in India 
and vaccines were not yet available.
