Possibility of Intrauterine Vertical Transmission of Coronavirus Disease 2019
(COVID-19): A Case Report from Iran.

Salehipour H(1), Yazdi ME(2), Abzevari MT(3), Shomoossi N(4), Rad M(5).

With the onset of Covid 19 disease, the vertical transmission of the disease from
mother to neonate was unknown. In this case, a mother affected by a severe Covid 
19 a few days before delivery, was examined whether her baby get the disease
without breastfeeding and close contact with his mother or not. Finally, the case
study show corona virus did not transmitted through blood from mother to the baby
and he was completely healthy.
