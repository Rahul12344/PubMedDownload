Hepatitis B virus RNA plays an important role in the process of hepatitis B virus
replication, especially pregenomic RNA (pgRNA), which serves as a template for
the viral capsids, so its particularity is increasing day by day. This article
analyzes the recent clinical studies, and further discusses the guiding
significance of HBV RNA as a new clinical serological marker for hepatitis B
patients.

Publisher: HBV RNA在乙型肝炎病毒复制过程中具有重要作用，尤其是前基因组RNA（pg
