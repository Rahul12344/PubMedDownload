Rapid sequence spinal anaesthesia: a technique reborn during the COVID-19
pandemic.

Patankar P(1), Mandour Y(1).

Author information: 
(1)Department of Anaesthesia, University College Hospital, London, UK.

With the wish to reduce aerosol generation and the shorter time to anaesthetic
readiness, this article discusses why rapid sequence spinal anaesthesia could be 
used in preference to general anaesthesia, for the benefit of both patients and
staff during the COVID-19 pandemic.
