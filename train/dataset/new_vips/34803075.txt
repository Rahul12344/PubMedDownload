Providing Food Assistance During the COVID-19 Pandemic: A Case Study of a Free
Produce Market at a Health Care Center.

Adams E, Babbin M, Bronico JV, Granick J, Weil R, Mitchell E, Carney C, Fiechtner
L, Mulugeta W, Zack RM.

The COVID-19 pandemic has worsened economic precarity and nearly doubled food
insecurity in the United States. We describe how a free produce market at a
Massachusetts health center adapted to exponentially increase its reach and
offerings while continuing to safely distribute food to a low-income community
during the pandemic.
