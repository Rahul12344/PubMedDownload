Implementation of an Isolation Shelter for Individuals with COVID 19 Experiencing
Homelessness.

Moss A, Gallagher T, Shaw B, Ferry-Rooney R, Manheimer C, Orlin K, Martinez M,
Delaney KR.

The COVID-19 pandemic had a devastating impact on non-sheltered homeless and
housing-insecure individuals. This report details the development of a
Chicago-based isolation shelter designed for people experiencing homelessness and
recovering from COVID-19. The model is informative concerning the rapid
development of services for people marginalized by the health care system.
