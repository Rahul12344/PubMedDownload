The National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI) produces a variety of
online information resources for biology, including the GenBank® nucleic acid
sequence database and the PubMed® database of citations and abstracts published
in life science journals. NCBI provides search and retrieval operations for most 
of these data from 35 distinct databases. The E-utilities serve as the
programming interface for the most of these databases. Resources receiving
significant updates in the past year include PubMed, PMC, Bookshelf, RefSeq, SRA,
Virus, dbSNP, dbVar, ClinicalTrials.gov, MMDB, iCn3D and PubChem. These resources
can be accessed through the NCBI home page at https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov.
