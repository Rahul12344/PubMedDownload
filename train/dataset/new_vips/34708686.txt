Incidence Trends for SARS-CoV-2 Alpha and Beta Variants, Finland, Spring 2021.

Kant R, Nguyen PT, Blomqvist S, Erdin M, Alburkat H, Suvanto M, Zakham F,
Salminen V, Olander V, Paloniemi M, Huhti L, Lehtinen S, Luukinen B, Jarva H,
Kallio-Kokko H, Kurkela S, Lappalainen M, Liimatainen H, Hannula S, Halkilahti J,
Ikonen J, Ikonen N, Helve O, Gunell M, Vuorinen T, Plyusnin I, Lindh E, Ellonen
P, Sironen T, Savolainen-Kopra C, Smura T, Vapalahti O.

Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 Alpha and Beta variants became
dominant in Finland in spring 2021 but had diminished by summer. We used
phylogenetic clustering to identify sources of spreading. We found that outbreaks
were mostly seeded by a few introductions, highlighting the importance of
surveillance and prevention policies.
