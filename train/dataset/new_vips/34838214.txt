[Intervention of the medical-psychological emergency unit with the carers during 
Covid-19].

The medical-psychological emergency units have been meeting with care teams to
discuss their experiences and feelings about Covid-19 for a little over a year.
This preventive approach allows for psycho-education and the identification of
stressful states.
