Drug Information Association (DIA) 2021 Global Annual Meeting (June 26-July 1,
2021 - Virtual Meeting).

Kaur R(1).

Author information: 
(1)Clarivate, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA. rajan.kaur@clarivate.com.

At the 57th Global Annual Meeting of the Drug Information Association (DIA),
attendees met virtually for the second time to support the theme of
'Collaboration without Boundaries.' Sessions included presenters and speakers
from regulatory agencies, patient advocacy and academia, with patients at the
forefront of discussions. This report covers a number of presentations and panel 
discussions from the 4-day meeting that primarily focused on the COVID-19 global 
pandemic.
