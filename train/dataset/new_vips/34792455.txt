Our Values Are Showing: Long-Term Care and the Pandemic.

Armstrong P(1).

Author information: 
(1)Distinguished Research Professor of Sociology, York University, Toronto, ON.

Given our diversity, it is risky to talk about "Canadian values," but this
examination of care for older people does reveal some prominent values evident in
how we provide care for this population. Identifying eight of these values, this 
paper argues that these values are being challenged in the wake of the COVID-19
pandemic.
