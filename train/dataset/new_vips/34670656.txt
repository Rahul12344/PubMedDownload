Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza A(H5N1) Virus in Wild Red Foxes, the
Netherlands, 2021.

Rijks JM, Hesselink H, Lollinga P, Wesselman R, Prins P, Weesendorp E, Engelsma
M, Heutink R, Harders F, Kik M, Rozendaal H, van den Kerkhof H, Beerens N.

We detected infection with highly pathogenic avian influenza A(H5N1) virus clade 
2.3.4.4b in 2 red fox (Vulpes vulpes) cubs found in the wild with neurologic
signs in the Netherlands. The virus is related to avian influenza viruses found
in wild birds in the same area.
