Coding-Complete Sequences of Recombinant Lumpy Skin Disease Viruses Collected in 
2020 from Four Outbreaks in Northern Vietnam.

Mathijs E(1), Vandenbussche F(1), Nguyen L(2), Aerts L(1), Nguyen T(3), De Leeuw 
I(1), Quang M(2), Nguyen HD(3), Philips W(1), Dam TV(3), Haegeman A(1), Van Borm 
S(1), De Clercq K(1).

Author information: 
(1)Sciensano, Exotic Viruses and Particular Diseases Unit, Ukkel, Belgium.
(2)Department of Animal Health (DAH), Ministry of Agriculture and Rural
Development, Phuong Dinh, Dong Da, Hanoi, Vietnam.
(3)National Centre For Veterinary Diagnostics (NCVD), Phuong Mai, Dong Da, Hanoi,
Vietnam.

Lumpy skin disease virus (LSDV) causes a severe, systemic, and economically
important disease in cattle. Here, we report coding-complete sequences of
recombinant LSDVs from four outbreaks in October and November 2020 in
northeastern Vietnam.
