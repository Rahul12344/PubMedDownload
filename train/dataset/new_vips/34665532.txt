The paper presents a review of publications concerning issues of development of
telemedicine in rural health care of the USA during in conditions of COVID-19
pandemic and expediency of extensive application of telemedicine technologies
during "post-COVID" period. The issues, aspects, prospects and required
conditions for extensive diffusion of telemedicine services to the rural
population are considered.
