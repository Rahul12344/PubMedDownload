#COVIDisAirborne: AI-Enabled Multiscale Computational Microscopy of Delta
SARS-CoV-2 in a Respiratory Aerosol.

We seek to completely revise current models of airborne transmission of
respiratory viruses by providing never-before-seen atomic-level views of the
SARS-CoV-2 virus within a respiratory aerosol. Our work dramatically extends the 
capabilities of multiscale computational microscopy to address the significant
gaps that exist in current experimental methods, which are limited in their
ability to interrogate aerosols at the atomic/molecular level and thus ob-scure
our understanding of airborne transmission. We demonstrate how our integrated
data-driven platform provides a new way of exploring the composition, structure, 
and dynamics of aerosols and aerosolized viruses, while driving simulation method
development along several important axes. We present a series of initial
scientific discoveries for the SARS-CoV-2 Delta variant, noting that the full
scientific impact of this work has yet to be realized.ACM Reference Format:
