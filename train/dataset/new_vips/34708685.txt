SARS-CoV-2 Variants, South Sudan, January-March 2021.

Bugembe DL, Phan MVT, Abias AG, Ayei J, Deng LL, Lako RLL, Rumunu J, Kaleebu P,
Wamala JF, Hm JJ, Lodiongo DK, Bunga S, Cotten M.

As the coronavirus pandemic continues, severe acute respiratory syndrome
coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) sequence data are required to inform vaccine efforts. 
We provide SARS-CoV-2 sequence data from South Sudan and document the dominance
of SARS-CoV-2 lineage B.1.525 (Eta variant) during the country's second wave of
infection.
