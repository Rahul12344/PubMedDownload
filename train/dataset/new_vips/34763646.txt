Case report of mild form of a new coronavirus infection in patient with
idiopathic pulmonary hypertension.

Gratsianskaya SE(1), Demchenkova AY(1), Martynyuk TV(1), Veselova TN(1), Ternovoy
SK(1).

Author information: 
(1)National medical research center of cardiology, Ministry of Healthcare,
Moscow.

The article presents a clinical case of mild novel coronavirus infection COVID-19
complicated with bilateral interstitial pneumonia in a female patient with
idiopathic pulmonary hypertension.
