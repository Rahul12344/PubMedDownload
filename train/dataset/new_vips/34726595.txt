Postmortem Antigen-Detecting Rapid Diagnostic Tests to Predict Infectivity of
SARS-CoV-2-Associated Deaths.

Heinrich F, Schröder AS, Gerberding AL, Gerling M, Langenwalder F, Lange P,
Heinemann A, Bibiza-Freiwald E, Nörz DS, Aepfelbacher M, Pfefferle S, Ondruschka 
B, Lütgehetmann M.

We investigated the infectivity of 128 severe acute respiratory disease
coronavirus 2-associated deaths and evaluated predictive values of standard
diagnostic procedures. Maintained infectivity (20%) did not correlate with viral 
RNA loads but correlated well with anti-S antibody levels. Sensitivity >90% for
antigen-detecting rapid diagnostic tests supports their usefulness for
assessment.
