severe fever thrombocytopenia syndrome (sfts) emerging disease caused 
by sfts virus (sftsv) family bunyaviridae. since virus first
isolated 2009, become widespread china, increasing number of
cases year year. although disease researched extensively past 
years, still effective measures suppress epidemic situation. 
this article reports pilot study comprehensive measures, including health
education risk communication, weed removal, livestock management, tick
control, prevent emerging disease endemic region china. the
density ticks decreased dramatically month month acaricides were
sprayed areas surrounding recreational agricultural settings. the
number sfts cases villages involved declined years the
integrated measures applied. comprehensive measures, especially
community-based tick control, may promising means preventing sfts in
endemic regions.
