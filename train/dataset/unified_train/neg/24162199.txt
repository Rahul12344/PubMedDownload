one hundred ten markers analysed linkage 218 f2 plants derived
from two divergent cultivars ('védrantais' 'songwhan charmi') cucumis melo
(l.). thirty-four rflps, 64 rapds, one isozyme, four disease resistance markers
and one morphological marker used construct genetic map spanning 14
linkage groups covering 1390 cm melon genome. rapd rflp markers
detected similar polymorphism levels. rflps largely due base
substitutions rather insertion/deletions. twelve percent markers showed
distorted segregation. phenotypic markers consisted two resistance genes
against fusarium wilt (fom-1 fom-2), one gene (nsv) controlling the
resistance melon necrotic spot virus, one gene (vat) conferring resistance 
aphis gossypii, recessive gene carpel numbers (3 vs 5 carpels: p).
