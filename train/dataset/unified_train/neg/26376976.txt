background: k-mer approach, treating genomic sequences simple characters
and counting relative abundance string upon fixed k, been
extensively applied phylogeny inference genome assembly, annotation, and
comparison.
results: meet increasing demands comparing large genome sequences to
promote use k-mer approach, develop versatile database, kgcak (
http://kgcak.big.ac.cn/kgcak/ ), containing ~8,000 genomes include genome
sequences diverse life forms (viruses, prokaryotes, protists, animals, and
plants) cellular organelles eukaryotic lineages. builds phylogeny based
on genomic elements alignment-free fashion provides in-depth data
processing enabling users compare complexity genome sequences based 
k-mer distribution.
conclusion: hope kgcak becomes powerful tool exploring relationship
within among groups species tree life based genomic data.
