an adult presented next day chickenpox. since majority the
patients extremely premature, concerned might have
received transplacental varicella-zoster virus (vzv) antibody, consequently
would risk contracting chickenpox. serum samples obtained from
each infant examined presence absence vzv antibody. fourteen of
the 16 infants antibody levels equal excess 1:16 indirect
fluorescent antibody, including six eight infants birthweights equal to
or less 1000 gm, eight ten infants gestational ages equal 
less 28 weeks. passive transfer immunity vzv appears common in
infants young 24 28 weeks' gestation.
