[construction screening recombinant fowlpox virus expressing eimeria
tenella f2 hybrid strain so7 gene].

[article chinese]

yang gl(1), zhang xc, zhao q, li jh, yin jg.

author information: 
(1)college animal science veterinary medicine, jilin university, changchun
130062, china.

objective: construct recombinant fowlpox virus expressing eimeria tenella 
f2 hybrid strain so7 gene.
methods: recombinant expression plasmid puta-so7 constructed inserting
the so7 gene eimeria tenella f2 hybrid strain downstream hybrid
poxvirus promoter flanked tk gene fowlpox virus (fpv). the
constructed puta-so7 firstly transfected chicken embryo fibroblast
cells(cef) pre-infected fpv strain 282e4 using liposome, viruses
resulted transfection selected 2 passages culturing cef
cells mem medium containing 40 mg/l 5-bromo-2-deoxy-uridine (brdu). the
selected viruses plaque-purified cef cultured mem medium without
brdu. results polymerase chain reaction (pcr), indirect immunofluorescence assay 
and western blotting showed s07 gene expressed recombinant fowlpox
virus.
conclusion: recombinant fpv (rfpv) expressing so7 gene obtained.
