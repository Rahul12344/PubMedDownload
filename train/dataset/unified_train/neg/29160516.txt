introduction: prevalence hepatitis e virus (hev) infection associated
factors investigated rural settlements central brazil.
methods: total 464 settlers interviewed, serum samples tested 
for anti-hev igg/igm. positive samples tested hev rna.
results: sixteen participants (3.4%; 95% ci 2.0-5.7) positive anti-hev
igg. none positive anti-hev igm. hev rna detected. dwelling 
rural settlement >5 years associated hev seropositivity.
conclusions: results revealed absence acute infection low
prevalence previous exposure hev.
