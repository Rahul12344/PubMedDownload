
1. virol j. 2012 oct 19;9:240. doi: 10.1186/1743-422x-9-240.

molecular detection novel paramyxovirus fruit bats indonesia.

sasaki m(1), setiyono a, handharyani e, rahmadani i, taha s, adiani s, subangkit 
m, sawa h, nakamura i, kimura t.

author information: 
(1)division molecular pathobiology, research center zoonosis control,
hokkaido university, sapporo, japan.

background: fruit bats known harbor zoonotic paramyxoviruses including
nipah, hendra, menangle viruses. aim study detect the
presence paramyxovirus rna fruit bats indonesia.
methods: rna samples obtained spleens 110 fruit bats collected
from four locations indonesia. samples screened semi-nested broad 
spectrum reverse transcription pcr targeting paramyxovirus polymerase (l)
genes.
results: semi-nested reverse transcription pcr detected five previously
unidentified paramyxoviruses six fruit bats. phylogenetic analysis showed
that virus sequences related henipavirus rubulavirus.
conclusions: study indicates presence novel paramyxoviruses among
fruit bat populations indonesia.
