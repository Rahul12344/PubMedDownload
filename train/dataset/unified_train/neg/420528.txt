reye's syndrome associated respiratory syncytial virus infection.

griffin n, keeling jw, tomlinson ah.

an upper respiratory tract infection 22-month-old boy followed rapid 
loss consciousness, hypoglycaemia, uraemia, death. necropsy examination
showed fatty change liver kidneys, severe cerebral oedema, bronchiolitis, 
and endocardial fibroelastosis affecting left ventricle. immunofluorescence
staining showed infection respiratory syncytial virus (rsv). clinical
and pathological findings reye's syndrome, previously reported 
accompanying rsv infection.
