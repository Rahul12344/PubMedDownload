quantification hiv dna brain pcr: differences fresh frozen
and formalin fixed tissue.

davison f(1), sf, scaravilli f.

author information: 
(1)department neuropathology, institute neurology, queen square, london.

hiv-1 dna extracted frozen formalin fixed brain tissue detected
using pcr. work extended amplifying, using semiquantitative pcr,
hiv dna extracted frontal lobe tissue 16 patients aids (eight
positive eight negative p24 antigen). dna amplified using hiv-1 pol
gene digoxigenin labelled primers detected chemiluminescence and
densitometry. cloned standards amplified parallel quantification. hiv
dna levels detected frozen tissue showed correlation p24 positivity and
the severity histological diagnosis. correlation less clear in
the formalin fixed material.
