acupuncture acute herpes zoster pain therapy (acuzoster) - design protocol
of randomised controlled trial.

fleckenstein j(1), kramer s, hoffrogge p, thoma s, lang pm, lehmeyer l, schober
gm, pfab f, ring j, weisenseel p, schotten kj, mansmann u, irnich d.

author information: 
(1)multidisciplinary pain centre, department anaesthesiology, university of
munich, munich, germany. johannes.fleckenstein@med.uni-muenchen.de

background: acute herpes zoster prevalent condition. one major
symptoms pain, highly influence patient's quality life. pain
therapy limited. acupuncture supposed soften neuropathic pain conditions
and might therefore act therapeutic alternative. objective present
study investigate whether 4 week semi-standardised acupuncture is
non-inferior sham laser acupuncture anticonvulsive drug gabapentine in
the treatment pain associated herpes zoster.
methods/design: three-armed, randomised, placebo-controlled trial total
follow-up time 6 months. estimated 336 patients (interim analyses) 
acute herpes zoster pain (vas > 30 mm) randomised one three groups 
(a) semi-standardised acupuncture (168 patients); (b) gabapentine with
individualised dosage 900-3600 mg/d (84 patients); (c) sham laser
acupuncture. intervention takes place 4 weeks, patients receive
analgesic therapy (non-opioid analgesics: metamizol paracetamol opioids:
tramadol morphine). therapy phase includes 4 weeks group (a) (c) 
consist 12 sessions per patient, (b) visits depend patients needs. main
outcome measure assess alteration pain intensity 1 week
after treatment sessions (visual analogue scale vas 0-100 mm). secondary outcome 
measure are: alteration pain intensity frequency pain attacks;
alteration different aspects pain evaluated standardised pain
questionnaires (npi, pdi, ses); effects quality life (sf 36); analgesic
demand; alteration sensoric perception systematic quantitative sensory
testing (qst); incidence postherpetic neuralgia; side effects cost
effectiveness. credibility treatments assessed.
discussion: study first large-scale randomised placebo controlled
trial evaluate efficacy acupuncture compared gabapentine sham
treatment provide valuable new information clinical and
physiological effects acupuncture gabapentine treatment acute
herpes zoster pain. study pragmatically designed ensure 
study findings implemented clinical practice acupuncture be
shown effective treatment strategy acute herpes zoster pain.
trial registration: nct00885586.
