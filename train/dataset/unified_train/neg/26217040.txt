dengue virus (denv) reintroduced peru 1990s reported
in puerto maldonado (population ~65,000) peruvian southern amazon basin
since 2000. region also highest human migration rate country,
mainly areas endemic denv. objective study assess
the proportion household income diverted costs incurred 
dengue illness compare expenses recent migrants (rms) and
long-term residents (ltrs). administered standardized questionnaire to
persons diagnosed dengue illness hospital santa rosa puerto maldonado 
from december 2012 march 2013. compared direct indirect medical costs
between rms ltrs. total 80 participants completed survey, 28
(35%) rms 52 (65%) ltrs. dengue illness episode cost the
household average us$105 (standard deviation [sd] = 107), representing 24% 
of monthly income. indirect costs greatest expense (us$56, sd =
87), especially lost wages. proportion household income diverted dengue
illness differ significantly rm ltr households. study
highlights significant financial burden incurred households family 
member suffers dengue illness.
