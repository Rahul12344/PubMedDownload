virus (a/whistling swan/shimane/499/83 (h5n3) air sac. inoculated
chicks showed mild severe diarrhea lesions pancreatitis atrophy 
the pancreas, thymus bursa fabricius. one chick died days 4, 6

kidneys, liver, lungs air sacs, cecal lamina propria. virus recovery
persisted longer pancreas. findings conformed of
stunting syndrome.
