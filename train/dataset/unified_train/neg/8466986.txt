an influenza virus, a/equine/alaska/1/91 (h3n8), isolated horses from
alaska acute respiratory infection. pathogenic serologic studies
revealed virus similar previously isolated equine h3n8 influenza 
viruses. antigenic analyses utilizing hemagglutination inhibition and
neuraminidase inhibition assays indicated antigenic drift prototype
equine h3n8 influenza virus, a/equine/miami/1/63. partial sequence analysis of
the a/equine/alaska influenza virus indicated 8 gene sequences are
of equine origin.
