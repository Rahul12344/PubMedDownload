[seroreactivity hepatitis b virus surface antigen among different population
groups city durango, mexico].

[article spanish]

alvarado-esquivel c(1), sifuentes-alvarez a, pérez-ochoa jf, garcía-corral n,
rodríguez-briones a, gonzález-castañeda jl, alonso-muñoz cm, bracho-huemoeller a.

author information: 
(1)facultad de medicina, universidad juárez del estado de durango, durango,
méxico. alvaradocosme@yahoo.com

objective: determine seroprevalence hepatitis b surface antigen (hbsag)
in several groups populations durango city, mexico.
methods: observational comparative study conducted 6 groups of
population total 775 persons durango city, mexico. groups studied 
were 141 registered female sex workers, 100 medical students, 150 blood donors,
104 persons applying medical certificates, 100 pregnant women, 180 drug
addicts. serum samples participants analyzed hbsag immunoassay.
hbsag confirmation performed neutralization assay.
results: 775 participants, 13 (1.7%) positive immunoassay,
and 1 (0.1%) resulted positive confirmatory assay. positive case
was drug addict history surgery national international
trips.
conclusions: seroprevalence hbsag several groups population in
durango city low; seroprevalence comparable lower those
informed mexican cities. strongly recommended perform hbsag
confirmation test due low specificity immunoassay.
