immunosuppression gb virus c-rna detection among hiv-infected patients in
cambodia.

sall aa(1), ségéral o, reynes jm, lay s, ouk v, hak cr, keo cl, lefait rr,
delfraissy jf, fontanet a.

author information: 
(1)institut pasteur de dakar, dakar, senegal.

in survey, 213 patients antiretroviral treatment programme phnom
penh, cambodia, tested gb virus c (gbv-c) rna treatment
initiation. advanced hiv infection, 34 cd4 cell counts > 200
cells/microl. gbv-c-rna detected 35 patients. proportion positive
gbv-c-rna decreased dramatically cd4 cell counts < 100 cells/microl. in
multivariate analysis, low cd4 cell counts, tuberculosis, anaemia, and
traditional medicine independently negatively associated gbv-c-rna 
detection.
