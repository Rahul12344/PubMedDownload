this case report describes clinical findings 22-year-old pregnant woman 
with confirmed zika virus infection, 16 weeks gestation, sucre,
colombia. ultrasound revealed severe oligohydramnios, intrauterine growth
restriction, complete absence urinary bladder fetus. poor
prognosis led decision terminate pregnancy. autopsy fetus
revealed severe bilateral renal hypoplasia.
