background: hiv hepatitis c (hcv) co-infection highly common among chinese
injection drug users difficult reach idus traditional vct
(voluntary hiv counseling treatment) clinics. new national model integrating
hiv/hcv testing methadone maintenance treatment started 2006. the
purpose study investigate hiv hcv test uptake associated
factors methadone clinics guangdong province, china.
methods: cross-sectional design using routine surveillance data laboratory 
testing confirmation applied determine rates hiv hcv test uptake.
multi-level modeling used examine individual-level clinic-level
correlates increased test uptake.
results: 45 49 methadone clinics guangdong province agreed to
participate study. among 13,270 individuals, 10,046 (75.7%) hiv
test uptake 10,404 (78.4%) hcv uptake. individual level, methadone
clients 30 years older likely hiv hcv test uptake (p
<0.001 both). clinic level, methadone clinics greater health care
personnel likely hiv (p = 0.01) hcv (p = 0.044) test
uptake. hiv test uptake significantly correlated hcv test uptake
(correlation coefficient = 0.64).
conclusion: methadone clinics provide opportunity routine integrated hiv
and hcv screening among drug users china. increased test uptake young drug 
users increased health care personnel clinics may improve
screening.
