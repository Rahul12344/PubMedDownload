nasopharyngeal lymphoid hyperplasia hiv carrier, mimicking nasopharyngeal
cancer.

author information: 
(1)department otolaryngology, taipei veterans general hospital, national yang

lymphoid hyperplasia cervical lymph nodes common head neck
presentation human immunodeficiency virus (hiv) infection. lymphoid
hyperplasia hiv carrier presenting nasopharyngeal tumor neck mass is
rarely discussed. reported 30-year-old male patient left side neck
mass 3 months. nasopharyngeal cancer neck metastasis impressed due
to huge nasopharyngeal tumor neck mass. patient transferred to
our clinic evaluation. physical examination revealed 30 x 30 mm
mass left jugulodigastric area 20 x 20 mm mass left posterior
triangle neck. telescopic examination revealed 20 x 20 mm bulging mass 
nasopharynx. nasopharyngeal biopsy done histopathologic examination
revealed lymphoid hyperplasia. laboratory survey showed seropositive hiv
infection enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (elisa) western blot
tests.
