the varicella-zoster virus major transactivator, ie62, contains potent
n-terminal acidic transcriptional activation domain (tad). experiments
revealed minimal ie62 tad encompasses amino acids (aa) 19 67. we
showed minimal tad interacts human mediator complex.
site-specific mutations revealed residues throughout minimal tad are
important activation mediator interaction. tad interacts
directly aa 402 590 med25 subunit, site-specific tad mutations
abolished interaction. two-dimensional nuclear magnetic resonance
spectroscopy revealed tad intrinsically unstructured. studies
suggest transactivation may involve tad adopting defined structure
upon binding med25.
