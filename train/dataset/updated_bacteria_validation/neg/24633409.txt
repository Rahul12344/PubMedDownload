Erratum in
    Nat Methods. 2015 Jul;12(7):692.

X-ray free-electron laser (XFEL) sources enable the use of crystallography to
solve three-dimensional macromolecular structures under native conditions and
without radiation damage. Results to date, however, have been limited by the
challenge of deriving accurate Bragg intensities from a heterogeneous population 
of microcrystals, while at the same time modeling the X-ray spectrum and detector
geometry. Here we present a computational approach designed to extract meaningful
high-resolution signals from fewer diffraction measurements.
