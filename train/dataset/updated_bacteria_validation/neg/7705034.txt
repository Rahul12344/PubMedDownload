Multiple-drug-resistant Salmonella gloucester infections in Bangladesh.

Hoque SS(1), Salam MA, Faruque AS, Albert MJ.

Author information: 
(1)International Centre for Diarrhoeal Disease Research, Bangladesh, Dhaka.

We describe three different cases of extraintestinal infections caused by
multidrug-resistant Salmonella gloucester from Bangladesh that were associated
with bad prognoses. These clinical manifestations were not previously reported
for S. gloucester.
