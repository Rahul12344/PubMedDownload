First case of Mycobacterium tuberculosis transmission by heart transplantation
from donor to recipient.

Weile J(1), Eickmeyer H, Dreier J, Liebke M, Fuchs U, Wittke JW, Richter E,
Gummert J, Knabbe C, Schulz U.

Author information: 
(1)Institute for Laboratory and Transfusion Medicine, Heart and Diabetes Center
North Rhine-Westphalia, Bad Oeynhausen, Germany. Electronic address:
jweile@hdz-nrw.de.

We report the first documented case of a Mycobacterium tuberculosis transmission 
by an orthotopic heart transplantation from the donor to the recipient.
Mycobacterium tuberculosis positive blood culture showed systemic prevalence of
the Mycobacteria, however, prophylactic therapy was able to prevent a clinical
manifestation of tuberculosis in the recipient.
