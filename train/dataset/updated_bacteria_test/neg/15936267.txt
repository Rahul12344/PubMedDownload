Innate immunity: NKT cells in the spotlight.

Van Kaer L(1), Joyce S.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Microbiology and Immunology, Vanderbilt University School of
Medicine, Nashville, Tennessee 37232, USA. luc.van.kaer@vanderbilt.edu

Cells of the innate immune system provide a first line of defense against
microbial invaders. Recent studies have revealed how one intriguing member of the
innate immune system, the natural killer T cell, is activated during bacterial
infections.
