
1. Zhongguo Zhong Yao Za Zhi. 1995 Dec;20(12):725-8, 762.

[Technology of processing carbonized root of Sanguisorba of ficinalis L. and the 
quality standards for its prepared pieces].

[Article in Chinese]

Ding A(1), Xiang Y, Li J, Rong J.

Author information: 
(1)Nanjing College of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

The technology of processing carbonized root of Sanguisorba of ficinalis was
selected using orthogonal experiment design. The result shows that the best way
is to stir-fry the drug in a pan for 7.5 min at 250 degrees C. The contents of
trace elements in the prepared piece are significantly increased, and the
prepared piece have marked hemostatic and bacteriostastic effects.
