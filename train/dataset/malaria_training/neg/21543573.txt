Combined deletions of pfhrp2 and pfhrp3 genes result in Plasmodium falciparum
malaria false-negative rapid diagnostic test.

Houzé S(1), Hubert V, Le Pessec G, Le Bras J, Clain J.

Author information: 
(1)Laboratoire de Parasitologie, AP-HP, Hôpital Bichat, Paris, France.
sandrine.houze@bch.aphp.fr

We report a case of misdiagnosis of Plasmodium falciparum malaria from Brazil
with negative PfHRP2-based rapid diagnostic tests (RDTs), leading to
inappropriate case management. Genetic tests showed the deletion of both pfhrp2
and pfhrp3 genes. The detection of two distinct P. falciparum target antigens is 
then advisable.
