
1. Emerg Infect Dis. 2018 Nov;24(11):2124-2126. doi: 10.3201/eid2411.180738.

No Plasmodium falciparum Chloroquine Resistance Transporter and Artemisinin
Resistance Mutations, Haiti.

Vincent JP, Komaki-Yasuda K, Existe AV, Boncy J, Kano S.

We obtained 78 human blood samples from areas in Haiti with high transmission of 
malaria and found no drug resistance-associated mutations in Plasmodium
falciparum chloroquine resistance transporter and Kelch 13 genes. We recommend
maintaining chloroquine as the first-line drug for malaria in Haiti.
Artemisinin-based therapy can be used as alternative therapy.
