Clinical, Virologic, and Epidemiologic Characteristics of Dengue Outbreak, Dar es
Salaam, Tanzania, 2014.

Vairo F, Mboera LE, De Nardo P, Oriyo NM, Meschi S, Rumisha SF, Colavita F, Mhina
A, Carletti F, Mwakapeje E, Capobianchi MR, Castilletti C, Di Caro A, Nicastri E,
Malecela MN, Ippolito G.

We investigated a dengue outbreak in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, in 2014, that was
caused by dengue virus (DENV) serotype 2. DENV infection was present in 101
(20.9%) of 483 patients. Patient age and location of residence were associated
with infection. Seven (4.0%) of 176 patients were co-infected with malaria and
DENV.
