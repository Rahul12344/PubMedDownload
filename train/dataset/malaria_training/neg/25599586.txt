Evolving the world's most dangerous animal.

Weetman D(1), Clarkson CS(2).

Author information: 
(1)Vector Biology Department, Liverpool School of Tropical Medicine, Pembroke

The devastating consequences of malaria are well known but many mysteries remain 
about its key protagonists, a handful of Anopheles species. New work provides a
framework for solving such puzzles, by generation and analysis of whole genome
assemblies for 16 Anopheles species, with genomic flexibility a key emergent
theme.
