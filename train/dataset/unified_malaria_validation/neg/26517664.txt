cases china imported across southeast asia border, especially

diversity p. vivax region. paper, report first genome
sequencing p. vivax isolate (cmb-1) vivax malaria patient cmb. the
sequencing data aligned onto 96.43% p. vivax salvador reference
strain (sal i) genome 7.84-fold coverage well onto 98.32% 14 sal 
chromosomes. using de novo assembly approach, generated 8,541 scaffolds
and assembled total 27.1 mb sequence cmb-1 scaffolds. furthermore,
we identified 295 known vir genes, largest subtelomeric
multigene family malaria parasites. results provide important
foundation research p. vivax population genetics.
