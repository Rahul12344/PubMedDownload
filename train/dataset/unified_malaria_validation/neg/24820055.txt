h. catesbianae, 14 large subunit rdna fragments, 9 small subunit rdna

of plasmodium falciparum identified mitrochondrial genome
sequence h. catesbianae. although presence 3 protein-coding regions and
numerous putative rdna fragments feature typical apicomplexan
mitochondrial genomes, mitochondrial genome h. catesbianae possesses a
structure gene organization distinct among apicomplexa. is
the first complete mitochondrial genome sequence obtained apicomplexan
