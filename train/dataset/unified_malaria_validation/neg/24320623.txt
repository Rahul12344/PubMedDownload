histomonas meleagridis causative agent histomonosis, disease of
gallinaceous fowl characterized necrotic typhlitis, hepatitis, high
mortality. develop rapid sensitive method specific detection h.
meleagridis, assay based loop-mediated isothermal amplification (lamp)
targeting 18s rrna gene established. detection limit lamp
assay 10 copies standard plasmids containing 18s rrna gene fragment,
which superior classical pcr method. specificity tests revealed 
that cross-reaction protozoa trichomonas
gallinae, blastocytis sp, tetratrichomonas gallinarum, plasmodium gallinaceum,
toxoplasma gondii, eimeria tenella, leucocytozoon caulleryi leucocytozoon
sabrazesi. assay evaluated diagnostic utility using liver and
caeca samples collected suspected field cases, detection rate 100
and 97.92%, respectively. results indicate lamp assay may a
useful tool rapid detection identification h. meleagridis poultry.
