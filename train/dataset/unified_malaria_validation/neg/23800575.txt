helicase-dependent amplification assay blood plasmodium parasite detection
and species-level identification. assay amplifies 18s rrna gene fragment 
of plasmodium species uses species-specific probe pan-malarial
probe definitively identify plasmodium falciparum infectious
plasmodium species. amplicon-probe hybridization products detected a
disposable dipstick enclosed cassette. pan-malarial-positive p.
falciparum-negative result, additional test performed detect the
pan-malarial-positive band result presence plasmodium vivax.
the assay uses 2 μl human whole blood directly 50-μl amplification 
reaction, without pre-amplification processing. clinical performance of
the assay validated using 88 samples new york patients suspected of
malaria babesiosis. overall sensitivity assay 96.6% (95% ci,
87.3% 99.4%), specificity 100% (95% ci, 85.4% 100%), compared
with gold standard microscopy laboratory-developed molecular assay,
respectively. analytical sensitivity 50 copies dna per assay 200
parasites per microliter blood, assay detect samples with
parasitemia levels <1%. novel molecular diagnostic assay requires minimal
laboratory instrumentation uses un-processed blood input; be
readily performed field.
