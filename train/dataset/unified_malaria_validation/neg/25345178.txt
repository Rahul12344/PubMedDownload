[large trophozoites blood smear falciparum malaria: one case report].

[article chinese]

wang yb, kong xl, xu y, zhang y, li j, zhao cl, miao f, chen xx.

this paper reports one case atypical falciparum malaria imported africa, 
whose blood smear contains many large trophozoites, punctiform massive
brown pigment granules, body shape plasmodium similar of
plasmodium vivax plasmodium ovale. gene detection pcr, case 
was diagnosed falciparum malaria. large trophozoites rarely seen in
the peripheral blood non-severe falciparum malaria cases, much attention
should paid identification plasmodium falciparum plasmodia
in microscopic examinations.
