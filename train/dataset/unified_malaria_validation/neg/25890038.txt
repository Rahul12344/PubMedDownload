background: vivax malaria historically epidemic henan province china
and anopheles sinensis main vectors poor farming communities bare the
greatest burden disease. knockdown resistance an. sinensis one the
mechanisms resistance pyrethroids. present study, frequency
of mutations an. sinensis examined henan province, china.
methods: anopheles collected kaifeng, tongbai, tanghe, pingqiao, shihe, 
and yongcheng counties henan province 2013. molecular identification of
anopheles species conducted polymerase chain reaction (pcr) amplifying the
internal transcribed spacer 2 (its2). part iis6 domain para-type
sodium channel protein gene polymerase chain reaction-amplified directly 
sequenced. frequency geographic difference kdr gene mutant types were
analysed.
results: 208 anopheles received molecular identification, 169
(81.25%) an. sinensis, 25 (12.02%) anopheles yatsushiroensis, 12
(5.77%) anopheles lesteri. 325 bp fragment para-type sodium channel
gene including position 1014 successfully sequenced 139 anopheles, of
which 125 (89.93%) an. sinensis, 12 (8.63%) an. yatsushiroensis, 2
(1.44%) an. lesteri. molecular analyses revealed mutations existed 
at codon 1014 an. sinensis an. yatsushiroensis an. lesteri.
frequency kdr mutation 73.60% (92/125) population an. sinensis 
henan province, l1014f (ttt + ttc) allele frequencies accounted for
46.40% (58/125), higher l1014c(tgt) accounted for
27.20% (34/125) ( χ2 = 55.423, p < 0.001). frequency kdr mutation in
kaifeng county 100% (49/49), higher 37.93% (11/29) in
tongbai, 54.55% (6/11) pingqiao, 50.00% (3/3) shihe, 62.50% (10/16) 
yongcheng county, respectively (χ2 = 39.538, p < 0.001; χ2 = 24.298, p < 0.001;
χ2 = 25.913, p < 0.001; χ2 = 20.244, p < 0.001). 92.86% (13/14) frequency
of kdr mutation found tanghe county, higher tongbai
county (χ2 = 11.550, p = 0.0018).
conclusions: high frequency kdr gene mutations population an.
sinensis henan province found.
