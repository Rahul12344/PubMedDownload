background: nucleic acid testing major method used monitor hiv viral
load. commercial systems based real-time pcr assays available for
high-volume centralized laboratory testing, fully automated.
objectives study design: compared diagnostic performance the
hologic aptima hiv-1 quant dx assay (aptima) (based real-time tma) the
panther instrument, fully-automated random access platform, of, the
roche cobas ampliprep cobas taqman (cap/ctm) hiv-1 version 2.0 (based on
real-time pcr).
results: probit analysis replicate dilutions nibsc international hiv-1 
standard, gave lods 8.6 c/ml aptima 15.2 c/ml cap/ctm. the
agreement assays excellent measuring hiv rna calibrated
reference (κ=0.90, p<0.001) good measuring clinical samples (κ=0.62,
p<0.001). correlation among samples quantified two methods was
very good (r=0.95, p<0.001) mean difference values obtained
with two assays 0.02 log c/ml b non-b subtypes. vast majority
of results showed <0.5 log variance two assays (89%); one sample
showed results differed 1.0 log c/ml.
conclusion: performance new fully automated aptima assay adequate
for clinical monitoring hiv-1 rna infections treatment. aptima 
assay well suited routine laboratory use.
