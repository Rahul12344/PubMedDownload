sexual transmission hepatitis c virus (hcv) studied 30 partners to
anti-hcv positive multitransfused patients bleeding disorder. anti-hcv
elisa c-100 used screening test. positive results confirmed with
the first generation riba test. indeterminate samples tested also the
second generation riba verify positivity. time sexual exposure
added least 95 years. 29 partners anti-hcv seronegative. 1
partner anti-hcv indeterminate. thus sexual transmission hcv rare
event.
