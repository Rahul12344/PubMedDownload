in paper, argued fact that, far, even worst most
far-reaching epidemics-from plague athens 430 bc plague of
justinian 541/542 ad hong kong flu 1968/69-always finally petered 
out explained using manfred eigen's quasispecies concept: indeed, the
infectious agents, duplicating infected organisms, mutate
all time, infected organisms carry along quite multitude of
mutational variants or-in manfred eigen's terms-a whole quasispecies of
infectious agents implying that, within quasispecies, variants that
differ wild type may actually serve kind vaccination program 
when infecting previously uninfected persons. context, data
regarding various recent epidemics also illustrated, using daniel huson's
splitstree software tool.
