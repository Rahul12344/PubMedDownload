objectives: november 20, 2016 april 17, 2017, outbreaks highly
pathogenic avian influenza (hpai) a/h5n6 occurred poultry farms gyeonggi
province republic korea. serosurvey conducted among poultry
farmers identify transmission hpai a/h5n6 virus humans.
methods: descriptive study 870 poultry farmers gyeonggi province korea
was conducted 2016-2017 outbreaks. serological testing performed
using microneutralization (mn) assay antibodies influenza
a/duck/es2/korea/2016 virus, antigenic properties similar of
the hpai a/h5n6 virus caused poultry outbreak.
results: overall, 523 exposed poultry farmers assessed serological
testing. consequently, tested negative hpai a/h5n6 virus via mn assay.
conclusions: based serological assays, transmission hpai a/h5n6 to
humans identified study cohort. additional studies be
conducted determine possibility poultry-to-human transmission hpai
a/h5n6.
