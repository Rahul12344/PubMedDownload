child suffered minor scalding febrile response combined
attenuated virus immunization (measles, mumps rubella [mmr]). despite
vigorous efforts child died 26 days accident. suggested

turn, led ards. histologically lung presented peculiar change with
fibroblastic nodules, vessel wall inflammation signs observed ards.
