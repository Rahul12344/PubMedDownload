
1. world j gastroenterol. 2016 aug 7;22(29):6716-25. doi: 10.3748/wjg.v22.i29.6716.

hepatitis c virus g1b infection decreases number small low-density
lipoprotein particles.

kinoshita c(1), nagano t(1), seki n(1), tomita y(1), sugita t(1), aida y(1),
itagaki m(1), satoh k(1), sutoh s(1), abe h(1), tsubota a(1), aizawa y(1).

author information: 
(1)chika kinoshita, tomohisa nagano, nobuyoshi seki, yoichi tomita, tomonori
sugita, yuta aida, munenori itagaki, kenichi satoh, satoshi sutoh, hiroshi abe,
yoshio aizawa, department gastroenterology hepatology internal medicine of
jikei university katsushika medical center, tokyo 125-8506, japan.

aim: investigate hepatitis c virus (hcv) g1b infection influences the
particle number lipoproteins.
methods: numbers lipoprotein particles fasting sera 173 japanese
subjects, 82 active hcv g1b infection (active hcv group) 91 cleared
hcv infection (svr group), examined. serum lipoprotein fractionated by
high-performance liquid chromatography twenty fractions. cholesterol and
triglyceride concentrations fraction measured using liposearch. 
number lipoprotein particles fraction calculated using newly
developed algorithm, relationship chronic hcv g1b infection and
the lipoprotein particle number determined multiple linear regression
analysis.
results: median number low-density lipoprotein (ldl) particles was
significantly lower active hcv group [1182 nmol/l, interquartile range
(iqr): 444 nmol/l] svr group (1363 nmol/l, iqr: 472 nmol/l, p <
0.001), high-density lipoprotein (hdl) particles (14168 nmol/l vs 
15054 nmol/l, iqr: 4114 nmol/l vs 3385 nmol/l, p = 0.042). number very
low-density lipoprotein (vldl) particles similar two groups.
among four ldl sub-fractions, number large ldl particles similar
between two groups. however, numbers medium (median: 533.0 nmol/l,
iqr: 214.7 nmol/l vs median: 633.5 nmol/l, iqr: 229.6 nmol/l, p < 0.001), small
(median: 190.9 nmol/l, iqr: 152.4 nmol/l vs median: 263.2 nmol/l, iqr: 159.9
nmol/l; p < 0.001), small ldl particles (median: 103.5 nmol/l, iqr: 66.8
nmol/l vs median: 139.3 nmol/l, iqr: 67.3 nmol/l, p < 0.001) significantly
lower active hcv group svr group, respectively. multiple
linear regression analysis indicated association hcv g1b infection and
the decreased numbers medium, small, small ldl particles. however,
active hcv infection affect number large ldl particles any
sub-fractions vldl hdl particles.
conclusion: hcv g1b infection decreases numbers medium, small, very
small ldl particles.
