a healthy-looking 44-year-old female admitted hospital complaining 
fever hemosputum. chest roentgenogram admission showed patchy
infiltrates segment 3 8 right lung. laboratory studies showed a
leukocyte count 9700/microliters, erythrocytes sedimentation rate 55
mm/hour c reactive protein 8.7 mg/dl. arterial po2 71.9 torr while
the patient breathing room air. transtracheal aspiration performed on
admission, strains culture bacteria, acid fast bacilli, fungi and
mycoplasma negative. respiratory syncytial virus isolated from
transtracheal aspirates. rsv complement fixing antibody titers rose 1:40
to 1:16. became afebrile fourth day admission chest
roentgenogram improved gradually. rsv infection considered the
differential diagnosis atypical adult pneumonias.
