(cbmv)--a new species genus carmovirus family tombusviridae.

complete genomic sequence viral rna calibrachoa mottle virus (cbmv) has
been determined. cbmv genome positive-sense single-stranded rna 3919
nucleotides length encodes five open reading frames (orfs). orf1 encodes a
protein predicted molecular weight 28 kda (p28). orf2 extends the
amber stop codon orf1 give protein predicted molecular weight of
87 kda (p87). readthrough domain p87 contains gdd motif common to
rna-dependent rna polymerases (rdrp). orf3 orf4 encode two small overlapping 
polypeptides 8 kda (p8) 9 kda (p9), respectively. 3'-proximal orf5
encodes capsid protein (cp) 37 kda (p37). untranslated 5'- and
3'-terminal regions composed 34 234 non-coding nucleotides,
respectively. comparisons amino acid sequences orfs cbmv with
members tombusviridae show cbmv closely related members the
genus carmovirus. phylogenetic analyses based amino acid sequences rdrp
and coat protein nucleotide sequences whole genome reveal cbmv
forms subgroup several carmoviruses. therefore, genome organization,
physico-chemical properties, sequence alignments phylogenetic analysis
support classification cbmv new species genus carmovirus,
family tombusviridae.
