respiratory diseases ranging mild illness fatal disease. natural killer
(nk) cells innate immune component kill infected cells secrete
cytokines modulate adaptive immune system; constitute first-line 
defense play important roles controlling iav infection. study
evaluated effect daily administration heat-treated lactobacillus
plantarum nf1-fortified yogurt immunity protection iav infection.

elevated nk cell-related cytokine expression levels. daily administration 
l. plantarum nf1-fortified yogurt iav infection also enhanced splenic nk
activity, lung inflammatory cytokine responses, survival rate. thus, daily
administration nf1-fortified yogurt enhances host immunity helps prevent
