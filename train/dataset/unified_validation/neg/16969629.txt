zucchini yellow fleck virus (zyfv, genus potyvirus) infects cultivated wild
cucurbits mediterranean basin occasionally causes severe damage in
crops. biological serological data tend indicate zyfv related to
other cucurbit-infecting potyviruses, mainly papaya ringspot virus (prsv) and
moroccan watermelon mosaic virus (mwmv). order establish unambiguously 
taxonomic status zyfv, sequence 3' part genome -
encompassing cp coding region - two zyfv strains originating italy
and france obtained compared potyviruses. results obtained
indicate zyfv belongs distinct potyvirus species, related but
different prsv mwmv.
