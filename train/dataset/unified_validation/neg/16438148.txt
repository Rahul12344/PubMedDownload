anemia hiv-infected patients common clinical manifestation. report 
a 31-year-old thai male, hiv positive 6 years, harbor
any opportunistic infection, receiving highly active anti retroviral
therapy (haart) one month, developed severe anemia. investigation
revealed pure red cell aplasia, suspected secondary parvovirus b19 infection. 
this diagnosis confirmed detection parvovirus b19 dna serum.
he received blood transfusions supportive treatment continued haart to
improve immune status resolve anemia. case suggests that
parvovirus b19 infection considered possible cause anemia in
hiv-infected individuals.
