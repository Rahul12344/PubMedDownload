questions investigated: recent emergence arboviruses chikungunya
virus (chikv) zika virus (zikv) brazil posed threat human health 
and country's economy. outbreaks occur mainly tropical areas; however, 
increasing number cases observed rio grande sul (rs), the
southernmost state; therefore, surveillance arboviruses essential for
public health measures.
design: study, analyzed 1276 samples patients clinically
suspected arboviral diseases 2014 2016. demographic clinical data
were collected described; cases microcephaly associated congenital
infection analyzed.
essential findings: results show chikv zikv entered rs 2014 2015,
respectively, imported cases confirmed. autochthonous infections occurred in
2016 viruses, total 5 autochthonous cases chikv 44 for
zikv. patients older 21 years; main symptoms fever,
arthralgia, myalgia, headache; rash, conjunctivitis, pruritus also
reported zikv cases. three cases congenital zika syndrome confirmed in
our study, another 20 cases microcephaly associated congenital
infection confirmed (10 positive syphilis, 6 toxoplasmosis 4 for
cytomegalovirus).
main conclusions: considering co-circulation different arbovirus rs,
including dengue virus, chikv, zikv, presence aedes aegypti and
aedes albopictus area, surveillance patients infected viruses 
contributes control prevention diseases. practical
difficulties diagnosing infections discussed.
