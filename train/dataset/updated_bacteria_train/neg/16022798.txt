
1. Emerg Infect Dis. 2005 Jul;11(7):1131-3.

Bordetella petrii clinical isolate.

Fry NK(1), Duncan J, Malnick H, Warner M, Smith AJ, Jackson MS, Ayoub A.

Author information: 
(1)Health Protection Agency, Respiratory and Systemic Infection Laboratory,
Centre for Infections, London, United Kingdom. Norman.Fry@HPA.org.uk

We describe the first clinical isolate of Bordetella petrii from a patient with
mandibular osteomyelitis. The only previously documented isolation of B. petrii
occurred after the initial culture of a single strain from an environmental
source.
