Cyclospora cayetanensis: this emerging protozoan pathogen in Mexico.

A Mexican airline pilot had clinical manifestations of illness after a five-day
stay in Lima, Peru. Six months later in Mexico, he was given a diagnosis of
infection with Cyclospora cayetanensis by using coproparasitoscopic serial tests.
He was treated twice with nitazoxadine successfully.
