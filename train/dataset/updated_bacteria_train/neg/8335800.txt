Diagnosing and treating halitosis.

McDowell JD(1), Kassebaum DK.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Diagnostic and Developmental Sciences, University of Colorado
School of Dentistry, Denver 80262.

Halitosis (bad breath) has been defined as offensive odors emitted from the
mouth. Although bad breath is a common complaint, identifying the cause and
developing an appropriate treatment plan can be difficult. A thorough examination
must be performed to rule out an oral source.
