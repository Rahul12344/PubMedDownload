
1. Tijdschr Diergeneeskd. 1995 Dec 1;120(23):683-5.

[Salmonella dublin infections on a goat farm].

[Article in Dutch]

Visser IJ(1), Vellema P, Hens IM.

Author information: 
(1)Gezondheidsdienst voor Dieren, Drachten.

A Salmonella Dublin infection in young goats has been described. In a period of
two weeks, 16 of 70 female-kids died within one day, because of septicaemia. Bad 
hygiene and management measures were discussed as the main reason for this
outbreak.
