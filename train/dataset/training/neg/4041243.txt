[Comparative evaluation of the diagnostic specificity and sensitivity of third
generation tests by the detection of hepatitis B virus markers in the serum. I.
HBsAg].

[Article in Italian]

Corridori S, Gorini P, Trespi G, Baldini E, Campisi D.

The authors have evaluated comparatively the diagnostic reliability of the most
recent RIA and ELISA mono-polyclonal techniques for the determination of HBsAg in
serum by means of in vivo and in vitro tests. Statistically, no difference has
been found as regards the various methods.
