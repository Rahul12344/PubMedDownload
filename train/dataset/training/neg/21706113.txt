Detection of viral nucleoprotein binding to anti-influenza aptamers via SERS.

Negri P(1), Kage A, Nitsche A, Naumann D, Dluhy RA.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Chemistry, University of Georgia, Athens, GA 30602, USA.
pnegri@uga.edu

A highly sensitive surface-enhanced Raman (SERS)-based method for detection of
influenza viral nucleoproteins is described. The intrinsic SERS spectrum of the
aptamer-nucleoprotein complex provides direct evidence of binding between a
polyvalent anti-influenza aptamer and the nucleoproteins of three influenza
strains.
