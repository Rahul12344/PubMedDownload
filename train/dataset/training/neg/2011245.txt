
1. Neurology. 1991 Apr;41(4):498-500.

'Locked-in syndrome' for 27 years following a viral illness: clinical and
pathologic findings.

Thadani VM(1), Rimm DL, Urquhart L, Fisher L, Williamson PD, Enriquez R, Kim JH, 
Levy LL.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Neurology, Yale University School of Medicine, New Haven, CT.

Comment in
    Neurology. 1991 Oct;41(10):1708.

We describe a man who, after a presumed encephalitic illness, was "locked-in" for
27 years. His CT and autopsy findings showed atrophy of the brainstem and a
cystic lesion at the base of the pons. He survived longer than most other
patients in a similar state.
