The new virus vB_BveP-Goe6 was isolated on the host organism Bacillus velezensis 
FZB42. The virus morphology indicated its association with the genus Phi29virus
The genome of vB_BveP-Goe6 (19,105 bp) comprises a linear chromosome with a GC
content of 39.99%. The genome harbors 26 putative protein-coding genes and a
noncoding packaging RNA.
