
1. Vrach Delo. 1990 Aug;(8):103-5.

[The diagnosis of Coxsackievirus infection in adults].

[Article in Russian]

Bondarev LS, Sotnik IuP.

Clinical manifestations were analyzed in 168 patients with Coxsackie infection
forms proved by laboratory tests. It is shown that it is possible to diagnose
Coxsackie infection forms on the basis of syndromes and their associations
characteristic of this disease.
