Epstein-Barr Infections in Adolescents and Young Adults.

Radetsky M(1), Overturf GD.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Pediatrics, University of New Mexico School of Medicine,
Albuquerque, NM 87131-5311, USA.

In the United States, Epstein-Barr virus is acquired primarily in late
adolescence. Acute infectious mononucleosis and other syndromes, complications,
and atypical infections associated with EBV are described, as are the
differential diagnosis, management, and treatment.
