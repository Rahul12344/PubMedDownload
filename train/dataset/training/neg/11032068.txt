Darier's disease complicated by Kaposi's varicelliform eruption due to herpes
simplex virus.

Pantazi V(1), Potouridou I, Katsarou A, Papadogiorgaki TH, Katsambas A.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Dermatology, University of Athens, A. Sygros' Hospital, Greece.

Darier's disease is an autosomal dominant dermatosis. It may rarely be
complicated by localized or widespread cutaneous viral infections. We describe a 
case of Kaposi's varicelliform eruption due to herpes simplex virus type 1
presenting in a patient with Darier's disease.
