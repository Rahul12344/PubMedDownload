
1. Ophthalmologica. 1978;177(3):134-9.

Pharyngoconjunctival fever caused by adenovirus type 19.

van Bijsterveld OP, de Jong JC, Muzerie CJ, Wermenbol AG.

A case of laboratory-acquired type 19 adenovirus infection is reported. The
patient showed the classical triad of pharyngitis, acute follicular
conjunctivitis and fever. Adenovirus type 19 was isolated from the affected eye, 
and the infection was confirmed by demonstration of seroconversion to this virus.
