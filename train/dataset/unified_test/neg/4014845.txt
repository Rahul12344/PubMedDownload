serologic classification two ovine adenovirus isolates central united
states.

adair bm, mckillop er, mcferran jb.

two ovine adenovirus (oav) strains (rts-42 rts-151), isolated lambs in
the central united states, compared using 2-way cross-neutralization tests
with 6 recognized oav species, 9 bovine adenovirus species, 4 porcine
species. virus rts-42 identified oav type 5, confirming previous results. 
virus rts-151 identified oav type 6, although serologic crossing was
largely one-sided.
