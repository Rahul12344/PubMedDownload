families: kirs ("killer cell immunoglobulin-like receptor") ilts
("immunoglobulin-like transcript"), mainly inhibitory binds hla class

cells. include molecules nkp30, nkp44, nkp46 et nkg2d. cellular stress
(viral bacterial infections, tumours) may modulate nk function different
mechanisms: decrease hla class molecules expression resulting lack of
engagement inhibitory receptors ultimately nk cell activation;
modulation cd94/nkg2a inhibitory function expression peptides

nk cells subsets alphabeta gammadelta cells. major nkg2d ligands
in humans mic ("mhc class related") molecules stress-inducible
during viral (cmv) bacterial infection (m. tuberculosis, e. coli). may 
also expressed tumors. therefore, could play role activating nk
and/or lymphocyte responses conditions.
