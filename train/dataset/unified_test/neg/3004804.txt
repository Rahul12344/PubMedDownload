the diagnosis enzootic bovine leukosis.

mammerickx m, portetelle d, burny a.

this paper reviews clinical virological diagnostic procedures for
enzootic bovine leukosis (ebl). clinical diagnosis must always confirmed
by specific laboratory test bovine leukaemia virus (blv). many virological 
tests proposed. sensitivity diagnostic methods sufficient 
to early detection blv infection individual base. advantages 
the highly sensitive methods like ria elisa appear samples be
tested naturally low antibody titers (individual milk, bulk milk,
pooled sera).
