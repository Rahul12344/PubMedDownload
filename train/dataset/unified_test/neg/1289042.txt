comparative investigation different turkey rhinotracheitis (trt) virus
isolates different countries.

the present investigation carried compare antigenic relationship
between trt virus isolates different countries. obtained results showed 
that virus isolates shared similar physiochemical properties. virus
neutralisation tests marked two way cross reactions 1 = 8544
(england), stg 761/88 stg 854/88 (germany) could detected. other
hand vco 3 isolate (france) showed partial reaction. also sds-poly
acrylamide gel electrophoresis (sds-page) profiles three trt viruses (one from
england two germany) similar, vco 3 strain from
france showed variation.
