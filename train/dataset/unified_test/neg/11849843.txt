the flower trollius chinensis bunge used treating upper respiratory
infections, pharyngitis, tonsillitis, bronchitis chinese folk medicine.
the antiviral activities crude extract, total flavonoids, orientin,
vitexin proglobeflowery acid isolated flowers t. chinensis
against parainfluenza type 3 (para 3) virus investigated. results showed
that crude extract total flavonoids exhibited weak antiviral activity
against para 3. orientin vitexin demonstrated potent moderate antiviral
activity para 3. proglobeflowery acid showed weak antiviral activity
against para 3.
