human clinical isolates influenza virus representing antigenic variants from
the last 15 years evaluated using rt-pcr assay.
results: amplicons 325, 198 365 base pairs length obtained from
rna extracted influenza a/h1n1, a/h3n2 b viruses, respectively. all
human-derived a/h1n1, a/h3n2, b reference strains antigenic variants
tested correctly identified.
conclusions: rt-pcr effective alternative traditional methods typing
and subtyping influenza viruses.
