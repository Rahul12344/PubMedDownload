genome/segment database. extracted 18 418 complete virus genomes/segments from
the international nucleotide sequence database collaboration (insdc,
http://www.insdc.org/) dna data bank japan (ddbj), embl genbank and
stored system. list registered viruses arranged
hierarchically according taxonomy. keyword searches performed for
genome/segment data biological features virus stored gib-v. gib-v is
equipped blast search function, search results displayed
graphically list form. moreover, blast results used online 
the clustalw feature ddbj. available virus genome/segment data 
collected gib-v download function. gib-v accessed charge at
http://gib-v.genes.nig.ac.jp/.
