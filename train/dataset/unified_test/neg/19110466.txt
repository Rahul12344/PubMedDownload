laboratory evaluation unicel dxi 800 analyser (beckman coulter) for
detecting hbv hcv serological markers.

background: due high prevalence, hepatitis b virus (hbv) hepatitis c
virus (hcv) infections need accurate rapid diagnosis tools.
objectives: technical performances unicel dxi 800 analyser (beckman
coulter) comparison vitros eci (ortho clinical diagnostics) were
performed five serological markers: hbsag, total anti-hbc, anti-hbc igm,
anti-hbs anti-hcv.
study design: reproducibility determined repeated tests the
manufacturers' controls. performance unicel dxi 800 assessed by
testing negative positive samples previously analysed vitros eci.
the accuracy linearity anti-hbs assay evaluated using who
international standard (w1042).
results: intra-assay inter-assay coefficients variation were: 0.8% and
4.4% hbsag, 2.4% 6.2% anti-hbc, 5% 8.7% anti-hbc igm, 2.1%
and 5.1% anti-hbs 3.7% 7.4% anti-hcv. two methods were
concordant: 100% agreement five markers except negative hbsag
sera (99%). anti-hbs results correlated well vitros eci (r=0.925
with p<0.0001) standard (r2=0.9996). throughput 216 tests/h.
conclusion: high throughput, specificity sensitivity make unicel dxi 800 
assays useful routine diagnoses hcv hbv infections.
