we report case sporadic acute type hepatitis associated acute renal
failure, due mesangioproliferative glomerulonephritis interstitial
nephritis. 42 year-old-man admitted mitsui memorial hospital 
jaundice oliguria fever february, 1989. serum creatinine 12.2
mg/dl, bun 87 mg/dl, got 57 u/l gpt 358 u/l. serum igm antibody to
hepatitis virus positive, indicated recent infection hepatitis a
virus. hemodialysis steroidal therapy started, patient's acute
renal failure liver dysfunction ameliorated within one month. light
microscopic examinations showed increased number mesangial cells an
increased amount mesangial matrix, also showed inflammatory cell invasion 
in interstitium. electron microscopic examinations showed proliferation of
mesangial cells matrix, dense deposit along basement membrane. on
immunofluoresent studies, fine granular deposits iga clq observed 
the mesangium.
