two distinct spherical virus-like particles purified brown
planthopper, nilaparvata lugens. one geographical isolate himetobi p
virus (cripavirus, dicistroviridae). 30 nm diameter and
contained positive-stranded rna. rna 1647 nucleotides length and
encoded capsid protein, indicating particle satellite
virus. transmission tests showed satellite transmitted vertically;
however, helper virus unknown. named satellite nilaparvata lugens
commensal x virus (nlcxv).
