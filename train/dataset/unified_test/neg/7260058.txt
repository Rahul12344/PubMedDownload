5-[(hydroxymethyl)-o-pyrophosphoryl]uracil, intermediate biosynthesis
of alpha-putrescinylthymine deoxyribonucleic acid bacteriophage phi w-14.

maltman kl, neuhard j, warren ra.

in nonpermissive host, amber mutant, 37, bacteriophage phi w-14
synthesizes deoxyribonucleic acid (dna) considerably greater buoyant density
than dna synthesized wild-type phage. 37 dna lacks the
hypermodified pyrimidine, alpha-putrescinylthymine (putthy). instead, contains
a new modified base, 5-[(hydroxymethyl)-o-pyrophosphoryl]uracil (hmppura).
extracts cells infected wild-type phi w-14 convert hmppura 37
dna putthy incubated putrescine.
