a unique case babesia gibsoni infected dog paraplegia.

a 4 year old male rottweiler presented teaching veterinary clinics of
gadvasu primary complaint paraplegia, fever inappetence. dog
was found dull elevated rectal temperature (105 °f), heart rate (158
per minute) respiration rate (57 per minute). blood smear examination pcr
assay revealed dog positive babesia gibsoni. elevated bilirubin,
alanine aminotransferase, alkaline phosphatase, creatinine, total leucocyte
count, neutrophils hypoglycaemia haemato-biochemical alterations. the
therapy targeting babesiosis carried single dose diminazene
aceturate combination doxycycline, enrofloxacin metronidazole for
21 days. complete follow case revealed complete clinical recovery
within 21 days.
