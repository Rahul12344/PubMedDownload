plasmodium knowlesi (a simian malarial parasite) infection resulted elevation 
of hepatic oxidative stress monkeys. further, antioxidant defence system
of host also noticeably affected. infected monkeys showed marked
increase levels superoxide (o2-), lipid peroxidation (lpo), glutathione
(gsh) xanthine oxidase (xo), decreased levels superoxide dismutase
(sod) catalase. oral administration chloroquine (20 mg kg body wt-1 3 
days) infected monkeys caused recovery trends oxidative stress and
antioxidant defences almost normal week cessation drug treatment.
