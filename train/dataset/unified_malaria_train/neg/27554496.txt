the terrestrial actinomycete strain bcc71188 identified streptomyces by
its morphology (having spiral chain spore aerial mycelium), chemotaxonomy 
(containing ll-diaminopimelic acid cell wall), 16s rrna gene sequence 
analysis [showing high similarity values compared streptomyces samsunensis
m1463t (99.85 %) streptomyces malaysiensis nbrc 16446t (99.40 %)]. crude 
extract exhibited antimalarial plasmodium falciparum (ic50 0.19 μg/ml),
anti-tb mycobacterial tuberculosis (mic 6.25 μg/ml), antibacterial
against bacillus cereus (mic 1.56 μg/ml) activities. therefore, chemical
investigation conducted employing bioassay-guided method led the
isolation 19 compounds including two cyclic peptides (1-2), five macrolides
(3-7), new naphthoquinone (8), nahuoic acid c (9), geldanamycin derivatives
(10-13), cyclooctatin (14), germicidins (15) c (16), actinoramide (17),
abierixin, 29-o-methylabierixin. isolated compounds evaluated 
antimicrobial activity, antimalarial, anti-tb, antibacterial
activities, cytotoxicity cancerous (mcf-7, kb, nci-h187) and
non-cancerous (vero) cells. compounds 1-7, 10-14 exhibited antimalarial (ic50
0.22-7.14 μg/ml), elaiophylin analogs (4-6) displayed anti-tb (mic
0.78-12.00 μg/ml) b. cereus (mic 0.78-3.13 μg/ml) activities. compounds 1, 2,
14, abierixin displayed weak cytotoxicity, indicating potential for
antimicrobial agents.
