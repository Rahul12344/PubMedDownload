objective: analyze genotypes homology msp-1 csp gene of
plasmodium vivax shandong province, provide evidence case
traceability.
methods: total 12 blood samples collected p. vivax-infected cases 
in shandong province 2011. parasite genomic dna extracted. primers were
designed according msp-1 csp gene sequences p. vivax. nested pcr, 
enzyme digestion, sequencing sequence alignment, homologous analysis were
performed.
results: msp-1 gene 12 samples p. vivax-infected cases were
detected 470 bp pcr amplification band, 350 bp 120 bp enzyme
digestion fragments, identified type sal-1. analysis of
phylogenetic tree msp-1 gene showed sequences 9 indigenous case
samples shandong province located branch, one case sample
infected india located branch india strains. 12
p. vivax-infected samples covered gdra (d/a) gqpa sequences csp gene, which
were identified type pv-ⅰ. csp gene among 12 p. vivax-infected samples,
10 samples indigenous case shandong province one sample case
infected guangdong province detected 560-840 bp 150-230 bp 
pcr amplification bands, identified temperate zone family strain of
type pv-ⅰ. however, one sample case infected india detected 
with 560-840 bp band, identified tropical zone family strain of
pv-ⅰ. analysis phylogenetic tree csp gene showed sequences of
10 samples indigenous cases shandong province one sample the
case infected guangdong province located branch, one sample
from case infected india located branch india and
indonesia strains.
conclusions: indigenous isolates shandong province, msp-1 gene is
genotyped type sal-1, csp gene genotyped temperate zone family strain type 
pv-ⅰ, high homology found among indigenous isolates.

publisher: ［摘要］ 目的 分析山东省间日疟原虫msp-1和csp基因类型及其同源性, 为病例溯源提供科学依据。 方法 采集
2011年山东省报告的12例间日疟患者血样, 提取疟原虫基因组dna; 分别根据间日疟原虫msp-1和csp基因序列设计 引物, 进行巢式pcr
扩增、酶切、测序、序列比对及同源性分析。 结果 12份间日疟患者血样msp-1基因全部出现470 bp扩增条带以及350、120 bp酶切片段,
均为sal-1型; msp-1进化树分析显示, 9份省内感染者样品序列同属一个分枝, 1
份印度感染者样品序列与印度分离株位于同一分枝。12份间日疟患者样品csp基因均包含gdra (d/a) gqpa序列, 为 pv-ⅰ型,
其中10份省内感染者和1份广东感染者样品csp基因出现560～840 bp和150～230 bp两种扩增条带, 为pv-ⅰ型温带族,
1份在印度感染者样品csp基因仅出现560～840 bp条带, 为pv-ⅰ型热带族。csp进化树表明, 10份省内 感染者及1份广东感染者样品序列同属一个分枝, 
1份在印度感染者样品序列与印度和印度尼西亚分离株位于同一分 枝。 结论 山东省本地感染间日疟原虫msp-1基因型均为sal-1型,
csp基因型均为pv-ⅰ型温带族, 本地虫株具有较强 的基因同源性。.
