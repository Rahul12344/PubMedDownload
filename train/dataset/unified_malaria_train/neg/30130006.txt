[analysis pfcrt gene polymorphism imported falciparum malaria cases in
henan province 2015].

[article chinese]

zhou rm, wang yn, qian d, li sh, liu y, yang cy, zhao yl, xu bl, zhang hw.

objective: analyze plasmodium falciparum chloroquine resistant transporter
(pfcrt) gene polymorphism imported falciparum malaria cases henan province 
in 2015.
methods: blood samples collected 132 cases imported falciparum
malaria henan province 2015. dna extracted samples, the
pfcrt amplified nested pcr using specific primers. pcr products were
digested restriction endonuclease enzyme apol sequenced. pfcrt gene
polymorphism distribution analyzed.
results: 132 cases imported malaria young male adults
returning africa, highest percentage west
africa（38.6%, 51/132）, central africa（26.5%, 35/132）, south africa（25.0%,
33/132）, east africa（8.3%, 11/132）, north africa（1.5%, 2/132）. nested pcr
yielded 145-bp product sample, 66.7%（88/132） products 
completely digested apol i, resulting two fragments 114 bp 31 bp;
32.6%（43/132） could digested single fragment 145 bp was
shown; 0.8%（1/132） incompletely digested, yielding three fragments of
145 bp, 114 bp 31 bp. blasting chloroquine sensitive strain 3d7,
we found mutations pfcrt sites correspondig residues 74, 75 76 
atg, aat aaa att, gaa aca （i.e. m74i, n75e k76t） 43 132 
blood samples, mixed type mutations atg/t, a/gaa/t aa/ca sites
correspondig residues 74, 75 76（cvm/i, n/e/d/k, t/k） one blood sample. 
the 88 blood samples showed wild type mutation (cvmnk). mutations
occurred mainly cases west africa（41.2%, 21/51）, east africa（36.4%, 
4/11）, south africa（30.3%, 10/33）, central africa（22.9%, 8/27）（χ2=4.07,
p＞0.05）. 2 cases north africa wild type pfcrt; one with
mixed type mutation west africa.
conclusion: three haplotypes pfcrt found, including wild type
(cvmnk), mutation type (cviet) mixed type (cvm/i, n/e/d/k, k/t) the
imported malaria cases. wild type occupies highest proportion （66.7%）,
while mutation type possesses high proportion 41.2% cases west
africa.
