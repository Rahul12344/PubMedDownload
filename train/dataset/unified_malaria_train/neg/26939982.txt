thelohanellus macrovacuolaris n. sp. described survey myxozoan
diversity common carp cyprinus carpio l. china. characterized 
presence round ellipsoidal plasmodium palate mouth host.
mature spores pyriform frontal view, lemon shaped lateral view,
measuring 21.6±0.9 (19.3-23.8) long, 12.5±0.7 (10.3-13.6) wide, 10.2±0.4
(9.8-11.8) thick. spores surrounded membrane sheath. single
polar capsule round apophysis top end presented close apex 
of spore, measuring 9.1±0.6 (8.0-10.0) length, 8.6±0.5 (7.8-9.6) width.
polar filaments coiled, 7 8 turns. large, round iodinophilous vacuole
was present, 5.8-7.5 diameter. present species morphologically
distinct thelohanellus species. blast search indicated that
the newly obtained small subunit ribosomal rna (ssrrna) gene sequence t.
macrovacuolaris n. sp. match available sequences genbank.
phylogenetically, t. macrovacuolaris n. sp. placed sister thelohanellus
wangi thelohanellus clade. morphology ssrrna gene sequence data
revealed present parasite new species genus thelohanellus.
