introduction: mutations propeller domain plasmodium falciparum
kelch13 (k13) gene associated artemisinin resistance.
methods: developed pcr protocol sequence pfk13 gene determined
its sequence batch 50 samples collected 2003 2016 brazil.
results: identified 1 k189t substitution located outside propeller domain 
of pfk13 protein 36% samples.
conclusions: although sample size relatively small, results suggest 
that p. falciparum artemisinin-resistant mutants exist brazil, thereby 
supporting continuation current treatment programs based on
artemisinin-based combination therapy.
