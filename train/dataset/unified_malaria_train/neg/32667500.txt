the aim study verify presence identify species of
haemosporidian parasites eared doves (zenaida auriculata) brazil. two
hundred eleven male female eared doves trap-captured four
different regions londrina city, southern brazil. whole blood collected
in edta tubes heart puncture euthanasia co2 chamber. nested 
pcr targeting mitochondrial cytochrome b gene (cyt b) haemoproteus
spp./plasmodium spp. performed, followed enzymatic digestion to
identify genus. phylogenetic trees constructed determine closely 
related species. 211 eared doves, 209 (99.05%) positive for
haemoproteus spp. and/or plasmodium spp. rflp analysis showed 72.72%
(152/209) eared doves positive haemoproteus spp., 6.22% (13/209)
were positive plasmodium spp., 21.05% (44/209) eared doves had
mixed infections. genetic analysis found four samples homologous with
haemoproteus multipigmentatus one homologous plasmodium sp.
this first molecular study hemoparasites eared doves brazil,
and also first description h. multipigmentatus plasmodium spp.
infection eared doves brazil.
