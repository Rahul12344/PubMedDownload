objective: develop new technique diagnosis plasmodium knowlesi at
the time able discriminate among diverse species plasmodium 
causing human malaria.
methods: study nested multiplex malaria pcr redesigned, targeting
the 18s rrna gene, identify fifth human plasmodium species, plasmodium
knowlesi, together human plasmodium (plasmodium falciparum,
plasmodium vivax, plasmodium ovale plasmodium malariae) amplified fragment
size using two amplification processes including internal reaction
control avoid false negatives.
results: technique validated 91 clinical samples obtained from
patients malaria compatible symptoms. technique showed high sensitivity 
(100%) specificity (96%) compared reference method
employed malaria diagnosis instituto de salud carlos ⅲ published
real-time pcr malaria assay.
conclusions: technique designed economical, sensitive specific
alternative current diagnosis methods. furthermore, method might tested
in knowlesi-malaria endemic areas higher number samples confirm 
quality method.
