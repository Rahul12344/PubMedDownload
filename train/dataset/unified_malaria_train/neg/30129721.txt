[species identification sequence analysis plasmodium spp. border areas 
of yunnan province 18s rrna-based nested pcr]

[article chinese]

zhang cl, zhou hn, nie rh, liu h, wang j, li cf, yang ym.

objective: analyze blood samples patients falciparum malaria and
vivax malaria border areas yunnan province, using 18s rrna-based nested
pcr, compare 18s rrna sequences.
methods: blood filter blood samples positive microscopic results for
plasmodium falciparum p. vivax infection collected laza, nankajiang,
mangdong nawei myanmar, mengla, tengchong yingjiang yunnan
province 2004 2011. 18s rrna-based nested pcr conducted the
samples, pcr products sequenced blasted. phylogenetic tree was
constructed using neighbor-joining method mega software (version 6.06).
results: microscopic examination revealed p. falciparum infection 256 samples 
and p. vivax infection 219 samples. 18s rrna-based pcr confirmed
p. falciparum infection 242 samples, p. vivax infection 176 samples, and
mixed infection 57 samples. consistency rate 81.7% （388/475） between
microscopic pcr results. inconsistency rate significantly correlated with
parasite density （spearman’s r=-0.408， p<0.05）. sequence alignment revealed 11
and 10 homologous sequences p. falciparum p. vivax 18s rrna gene,
comprising 2.9%（6/205） 22.5%（27/120） variable sites, respectively. 18s
rrna p. falciparum clustered cameroon（genbank accession number 
kc428742）, distantly related s-type 18s rrna the
netherlands （u36465） brazil （u36466 u36467）. 18s rrna p. vivax
clustered a-type 18s rrna thailand （u07367）, distantly related 
with c-type 18s rrna thailand（u07368）.
conclusion: nested pcr revealed mixed infection 57 samples among those
identified single infection microscopy. significant
difference 18s rrna sequence seven counties/cities yunnan province.
