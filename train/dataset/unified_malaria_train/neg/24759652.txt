haemoproteus ilanpapernai karadjian landau n. sp. spotted wood owl, 
strix seloputo, singapore described material ilan paperna's
collection slides. species previously identified haemoproteus
syrnii (mayer, 1910). however, comparisons material strix
seloputo material strix aluco, type host h. syrnii,
revealed morphological molecular differences. h. ilanpapernai n. sp. differs 
morphologically h. syrnii much smaller size gametocytes, the
different position mature gametocytes erythrocyte (apical,
subapical, lateral h. ilanpapernai vs. always lateral h. syrnii), the
effect erythrocyte nucleus (frequently tilted h. ilanpapernai not
displaced laterally vs. straight displaced laterally h. syrnii) and
characters pigment (aggregated gametocytes h. ilanpapernai vs.
dispersed h. syrnii). molecular analysis showed two species differ 
by 2.9% cyt b 3.1% coi genes.
