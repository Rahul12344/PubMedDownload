[polymorphism analysis plasmodium falciparum histidine-rich protein ii and
iii].

[article chinese]

yang yc, zhang j, wang gz, bo sy, zhang y, xin xf.

objective: analyze polymorphism plasmodium falciparum histidine-rich
protein (pfhrp) ii iii.
methods: genomic dna isolated blood samples 20 patients infected with
plasmodium falciparum yunnan province. blood samples tested microscopy
and rdts. pfhrp2 pfhrp3 gene fragments amplified pcr and
sequenced. sequencing results analyzed compared using the
bioinformatics software.
results: 20 patients infected plasmodium falciparum tested microscopy and
rdts. pcr showed pfhrp2 gene 389~986 bp, pfhrp3 gene with
329-640 bp. pfh-irp ii sequences started type 1 repeat (ahhahhvad) and
ended type 12 repeat (ahhaaahheaath). number type 7 (ahhaad),
type 2 (ahhahhaad) type 6 (ahhatd) within pfhrp ii other
types repeats, well type 16 (ahhaan) type 17 (ahhdg) pfhrp iii. 
type 11 repeat (ahn) found pfhrp ii pfhrp iii sequences.
conclusion: extensive diversity pfhrp2 pfhrp3 fragments the
individuals infected p. falciparum yunnan. types repeats are
shared pfhrp ii pfhrp iii.
