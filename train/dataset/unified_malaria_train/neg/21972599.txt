according sequences small subunit ribosomal rna (ssu rrna) gene of
plasmodium spp., universal species-specific primers designed detect
malaria identify species. 60 blood samples detected established
nested pcr method. results compared microscopic
examination. 40 blood samples plasmodium-positive nested pcr 22
samples p. falciparum, 13 p. vivas, 3 p. falciparum p. vivax mixed
infection, 1 p. ovale 1 unclassified malaria infection. altogether, the
coincidence results nested pcr microscopy stood 76.7%
(46/60), including 18 p. falciparum, 11 p. vivax 17 negatives. 
sequence analysis real-time pcr performed detect blood samples with
discrepancy, results nested pcr. amplified 
product p. ovale sequenced showed 100% homology corresponding
part p. ovale ssu rrna gene sequence (genbank no. dq845247), confirmed
that case imported ovale malaria.
