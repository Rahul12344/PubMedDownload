Further studies of a simian virus 40-like virus isolated from human brain.

Weiner LP, Herndon RM, Narayan O, Johnson RT.

A virus similar to simian virus 40 was reisolated from brain homogenates of a
patient with progressive multifocal leukoencephalopathy onto cultures of human
fetal brain cells.
