[Severe exacerbation of chronic hepatitis B following interferon therapy].

[Article in Polish]

Laskus T(1), Radkowski M, Cianciara J, Loch T, Slusarczyk J.

Author information: 
(1)Zakładu Immunopatologii Instytutu Chorób Zakaźnych i Pasozytniczych Ak. Med., 
Warszawie.

A patient with chronic active hepatitis B was treated with interferon alpha.
Inhibition of HBV replication was associated with severe exacerbation of
hepatitis. Treatment of chronic hepatitis B with interferon may lead to transient
decompensation of liver function, especially in patients with high activity of
morphological changes.
