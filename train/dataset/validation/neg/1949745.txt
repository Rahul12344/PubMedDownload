[Differential diagnostic principles and the classification of
infectious-inflammatory diseases of the nervous system].

[Article in Russian]

Golovchenko IuI.

The author presents data on multifactorial principles of classification and
differential diagnosis of infectious-inflammatory diseases of the nervous system 
with consideration of immune interaction of macro- and microorganisms, local
factors of infection and inflammation, etiological principles, microbiological
verification, bacteriology. The importance is stressed of pre-etiological
diagnosis of the acute period in determining the trends of pathogenetic
treatment.
