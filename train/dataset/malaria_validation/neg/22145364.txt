
1. Zh Mikrobiol Epidemiol Immunobiol. 2011 Sep-Oct;(5):115-21.

["Fingerprints" of malaria parasites in human genome].

[Article in Russian]

Sergiev VP, Filatov NN.

The effect of malaria epidemics on human genome alteration is discussed. Global
and regional features of human polymorphism are examined through the genetic
selection lens implemented by different malaria parasites species over the course
of Homo sapiens evolution.
