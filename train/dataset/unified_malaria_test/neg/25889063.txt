background: deficiency enzyme g6pd (g6pdd) caused mutations the
gene g6pd, plays important role protecting red blood cell against
oxidizing agents; linked chromosome x, may affects sexes.
the clinically relevant manifestations, acute haemolytic anaemia, mainly 
occur men, however. 8-aminoquinoline primaquine, medication
used radical treatment malaria caused plasmodium vivax, represents
the main factor triggers complications associated g6pdd. current
study aims estimate costs g6pdd among male individuals infected p.
vivax brazilian amazon.
methods: economic analysis developed within brazilian national
health system perspective years 2009, 2010 2011. direct medical
and non-medical costs estimated g6pdd brazilian amazon,
considering among suffering deficiency costs diagnosing
infection p. vivax, treatment severe adverse events require
hospitalization connected use primaquine.
results: estimates average costs diagnosing vivax malaria, its
treatment severe adverse events using primaquine among carriers 
of g6pdd, three evaluated years, corresponded us$ 739,410.42; us$
2,120.04 us$ 4,858,108.87, respectively. results indicate the
average total cost study period corresponded us$ 5,599,639.33, varying 
in accordance sensitivity analysis us$ 4,439,512.14 us$
6,702,619.24.
conclusion: results indicate use primaquine among men g6pdd 
who infected p. vivax represents heavy burden public health
service brazil.
