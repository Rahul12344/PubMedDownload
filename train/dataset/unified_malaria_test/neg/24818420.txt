liu y, zhou rm, chen wq, qian d, zhao yl, zhao xd, xu bl, su yp, zhang hw.

thirty-seven blood samples plasmodium vivax-infected patients collected
in henan province 2011. gene circumsporozoite protein (pvcsp) was
amplified nested pcr. according epidemiological data, among 37
plasmodium vivax malaria cases, 26 indigenous cases 11 imported
cases. fragment 750 bp obtained 37 blood samples. 750 bp pcr 
product digested restriction endonuclease alu-i mva-i, genetic
polymorphism investigated pcr-rflp. pcr-rflp analysis pvcsp revealed 
that parasites pv-i type (97.3%, 36/37) pv-ii type (2.7%, 1/37). 26 
samples indigenous cases exclusively pv-i type pv-i-b type (57.7%,
15/26) pv-i-a type (42.3%, 11/26). among 11 samples imported cases,
pv-i-a type accounted 90.9% (10/11), 1 pv-ii type accounted 9.1%
(1/11), pv-i-b type detected.
