background: chloroquine resistance plasmodium falciparum malaria been
associated pfcrt 76t (chloroquine resistance transporter gene) pfmdr1
86y (multidrug resistance gene 1) alleles. pfcrt 76t enables transport of
protonated chloroquine parasites digestive vacuole resulting loss
of hydrogen ions (h(+)). v type h(+) pyrophosphatase (pfvp2) thought pump
h(+) digestive vacuole. study aimed describe geographic
distribution single nucleotide polymorphisms pfvp2 possible
associations pfcrt pfmdr1 polymorphisms.
methods: blood samples 384 patients collected (1981-2009) honduras
(n=35), colombia (n=50), liberia (n=50), guinea bissau (n=50), tanzania (n=50),
iran (n=50), thailand (n=49) vanuatu (n=50) analysed. pfcrt 72-76
haplotype, pfmdr1 copy numbers, pfmdr1 n86y pfvp2 v405i, k582r p711s
alleles identified using pcr based methods.
results: pfvp2 amplified 344 samples. pfvp2 allele proportions were
v405 (97%), 405i (3%), k582 (99%), 582r (1%), p711 (97%) 711s (3%). the
number patients pfvp2 405i, 582r and/or 711s follows:
honduras (2/30), colombia (0/46), liberia (7/48), guinea-bissau (4/50), tanzania 
(3/48), iran (3/50), thailand (1/49) vanuatu (0/31). alleles most
common liberia (p=0.01) liberia+guinea-bissau (p=0.01). vkp haplotype 
was found 189/194 (97%) 131/145 (90%) samples harbouring pfcrt 76t and
pfcrt k76 respectively (p=0.007).
conclusions: vkp haplotype dominant. pfvp2 405i, 582r 711s snps 
were seen cq resistance highly prevalent time blood
sampling possibly due greater genetic variation prior bottle neck event
of spreading cq resistance. association pfvp2 vkp haplotype and
pfcrt 76t, may indicate pfvp2 involved cq resistance, should
therefore interpreted caution.
