withdrawal chloroquine burkina faso. total 675 clinical isolates
collected october 2010 september 2012 successfully genotyped. single
nucleotide polymorphism pfcrt (codon 76) gene analyzed. prevalence 
resistant pfcrt 76t allele 20.55%. progressive decrease the
proportion mutant type pfcrt t76 2010 2012 (x2=5.508 p=0.0189). our
results suggest progressive return wild type pfcrt k76 burkina faso
but prevalence mutants pfcrt t76 still remains high.
