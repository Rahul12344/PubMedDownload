Co-existence of Chiari malformation type I and Epstein-Barr virus
meningoencephalitis in a 3-year-old child: case report and review of the
literature.

Solomou EK(1), Krepis A, Kotsarini C, Badra FA, Patriarcheas G, Papanastasiou D.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Radiology, University Hospital of Patras, 265 00 Rio, Greece.
solomou@med.upatras.gr

In the present report we describe an unusual case of a 3-year-old girl who was
admitted to our hospital with Epstein-Barr virus meningoencephalitis. Brain
magnetic resonance imaging revealed diffuse abnormalities in white matter and
Chiari I malformation with cervical and thoracic hydro-syringomyelia.
