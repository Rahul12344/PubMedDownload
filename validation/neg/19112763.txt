
1. J Drugs Dermatol. 2008 Oct;7(10):972-3.

Psoriasis and psoriatic arthritis in a patient with HIV: response to
mycophenolate mofetil treatment.

Forman SB(1), Higginson R, Garrett AB.

Author information: 
(1)The University of Central Florida College of Medicine, Tampa, FL, USA.
sethbforman@gmail

A case of psoriasis and psoriatic arthritis in a 38-year-old white male patient
infected with human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) treated safely and effectively
with mycophenolate mofetil (MMF) is reported. Treatments for psoriasis and
psoriatic arthritis are manifold, including topical, oral, intramuscular,
intravenous, and subcutaneous therapies. These indicated treatments for psoriasis
and psoriatic arthritis result in suppression of the immune system.
