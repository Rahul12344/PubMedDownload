
1. An Esp Pediatr. 1988 Feb;28(2):137-40.

[Susceptibility of pregnant women to rubella and the implications of immunization
programs].

[Article in Spanish]

Villalbí JR(1), Graells C, Marín T.

Author information: 
(1)Centro Piloto de Asistencia Primaria Ciudad Badía, Instituto Catalánde la
Salud, Barcelona.

Rubella immunity in the sera of 331 pregnant women in Ciudad Badia (Catalonia,
Spain) is studied. No protection against rubella was found in 17% of them. No
significant differences were found among different age groups. Implications of
current vaccination policy are discussed.
