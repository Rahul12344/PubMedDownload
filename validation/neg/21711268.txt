Diagnosis of equine influenza virus infections in quarantine stations in
Australia, 2007.

Watson J(1), Selleck P, Axell A, Bruce K, Taylor T, Heine H, Daniels P, Jeggo M.

Author information: 
(1)Australian Animal Health Laboratory, CSIRO Livestock Industries, Geelong,
Victoria 3220, Australia. james.watson@csiro.au

In August 2007, several horses showed pyrexia and respiratory signs while in
post-arrival quarantine in Australia. Subsequent investigations diagnosed equine 
influenza by serology and PCR in two quarantine stations. A common origin in a
shipment of horses from Japan was indicated.
