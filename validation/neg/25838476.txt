Complete genome sequence of a tomato mottle mosaic virus isolate from the United 
States.

Fillmer K(1), Adkins S(2), Pongam P(1), D'Elia T(3).

Author information: 
(1)Department of Biology, Indian River State College, Fort Pierce, Florida, USA.
(2)U.S. Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Research Service, Horticultural
Research Laboratory, Fort Pierce, Florida, USA.
(3)Department of Biology, Indian River State College, Fort Pierce, Florida, USA
tdelia@irsc.edu.

Tomato mottle mosaic virus was recently reported from the United States following
its original description from Mexico as a novel Tobamovirus species. We present
the first complete genome sequence of a tomato mottle mosaic virus isolate from
the United States.
