[Typing of rotavirus strains using electrophoresis].

[Article in Serbian]

Jovicić V(1), Lepsanović Z, Krstić L.

Author information: 
(1)Vojnomedicinska akademija, Institut za mikrobiologiju ZPM.

Polyacrylamide gels electrophoresis was used in the analysis of rotavirus genome 
strains isolated in the regions of Belgrade and Nis in the period 1987-1991. Ten 
samples were examined and the same type of motility was found.
