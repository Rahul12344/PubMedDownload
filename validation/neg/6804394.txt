
1. Infect Immun. 1982 Apr;36(1):440-1.

Bacteriophages in sputum of patients with bronchopulmonary Pseudomonas
infections.

Tejedor C, Foulds J, Zasloff M.

Pseudomonas aeruginosa bacteriophages were recovered from the sputum of patients 
with chronic P. aeruginosa bronchopulmonary infection. Host ranges of some of the
phages are presented.
