Complete Genome Sequence of a New Member of the Marseilleviridae Recovered from
the Brackish Submarine Spring in the Cassis Port-Miou Calanque, France.

Marseilleviridae is a rapidly expanding family of Acanthamoeba-infecting large
DNA viruses distributed worldwide. We report here the complete 349-kbp genome
sequence of Port-Miou virus, which is surprisingly close to that of Lausannevirus
(isolated from the Seine River upstream from Paris, France), despite the strong
dissimilarities of their sampling locations.
